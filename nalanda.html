Basic Information

Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS) is a premier institution for social sciences education and research in India. Established in 1936 as the Sir Dorabji Tata Graduate School of Social Work, it was renamed to its current title in 1944. TISS was granted the status of Deemed University in 1964 and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. The institute has consistently been ranked among the top universities in India for social sciences by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

The Tata Institute of Social Sciences was established in 1936 as the Sir Dorabji Tata Graduate School of Social Work, the first school of social work in India. It was founded with the objective of creating a professional cadre of social workers to contribute to nation-building. In 1944, it was renamed as the Tata Institute of Social Sciences. The institute was granted deemed university status in 1964 by the University Grants Commission (UGC) of India. Over the years, TISS has expanded its academic programs and established campuses across India, including in Hyderabad, Guwahati, and Tuljapur, in addition to its main campus in Mumbai.

Vision and Mission

  • To be an institution of excellence in higher education that continually responds to changing social realities through the development and application of knowledge.
  • To create a people-centered society that promotes and protects dignity, equality, social justice, and human rights for all.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and collaborative learning.

Objectives

  • To develop and disseminate knowledge in social sciences through teaching, research, field action, and extension.
  • To contribute to the creation of a just and humane society through professional education and social intervention.
  • To promote critical thinking, democratic values, and social responsibility among students.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions for academic excellence and social development.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

TISS is renowned for its research programs across various disciplines in social sciences. The institute offers M.Phil and Ph.D. programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

M.Phil and Ph.D. Programs:

The M.Phil and Ph.D. programs at TISS are designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The programs involve coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Social Work: Community Development, Social Policy, Criminology and Justice

  • Health Studies: Public Health, Mental Health, Health Systems

Admission to the M.Phil and Ph.D. programs is based on the TISS Research Entrance Test followed by interviews. The institute provides fellowships and research assistantships to support research students during their studies.

üèï Campus Life

TISS has multiple campuses across India, with its main campus located in Mumbai. The Mumbai campus is spread over 10.5 acres in the Deonar area of the city. The institute also has campuses in Hyderabad, Guwahati, and Tuljapur. Each campus provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its blend of modern facilities and a focus on social justice, TISS offers a unique academic experience in the heart of India's financial capital.

Hostels and Accommodation

TISS provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The institute has several hostels for postgraduate and research students, separate for men and women. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at TISS is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration. The institute also provides accommodation for married students and international students.

Sports and Recreation

TISS encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports ground, and indoor games facilities. The institute also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year. Students participate in various sports competitions at the inter-university level.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at TISS is vibrant and diverse. The institute has several student committees and clubs that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Mosaic," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents. TISS also organizes various seminars, workshops, and conferences on social issues throughout the year.

Facilities and Amenities

TISS provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped classrooms, seminar halls, and research centers. The institute has a central library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources on social sciences. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at TISS is one of the best social sciences libraries in India with over 1,50,000 volumes.
