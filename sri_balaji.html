About the University

Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth Deemed University is a premier institution of higher education located in Puducherry, India. Established in 2008 under Section 3 of the UGC Act, the university has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution offering programs in medicine, dentistry, nursing, physiotherapy, and allied health sciences. The university is committed to excellence in education, research, and healthcare services, with a focus on developing competent healthcare professionals who can serve society with compassion and dedication.

Constituent Institutions

The university comprises several constituent institutions, each specializing in different areas of healthcare education:

  • Sri Venkateshwaraa Medical College and Hospital (SVMCH), Puducherry
  • Sri Lakshmi Narayana Institute of Medical Sciences (SLIMS), Puducherry
  • Shri Sathya Sai Medical College and Research Institute (SSSMCRI), Chennai
  • Sri Balaji Dental College and Hospital (SBDCH), Chennai
  • Sri Balaji College of Nursing, Puducherry
  • Sri Balaji Physiotherapy College and Research Institute, Puducherry

Each institution is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and provides comprehensive education and training in their respective fields.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a globally recognized center of excellence in health sciences education, research, and healthcare delivery.
  • To produce competent healthcare professionals with strong ethical values and commitment to serve humanity.
  • To promote research and innovation that addresses the health needs of society.
  • To provide accessible and affordable healthcare to all sections of society.

Core Values

  • Mandatory for admission to MBBS/BDS programs
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, English
  • Includes negative marking
1

2. Qualify NEET UG with Required Score

  • Higher scores required for competitive programs
  • Check university website for cutoff scores

3. Register for University Counselling

  • Visit official university website: balajividyapeeth.edu.in
  • Fill online application form with NEET details

  • Upload required documents

  • University conducts separate counselling for deemed universities

    • NEET rank
    • Category (General/OBC/SC/ST/EWS)

    • Availability of seats

    • Report to allotted college within timeframe

🏕️ Campus Life

Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth Deemed University has multiple campuses across Puducherry and Chennai, each providing a unique learning environment. The campuses are equipped with modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, well-stocked libraries, and excellent clinical training facilities. The serene environment of the campuses provides an ideal setting for learning and personal growth.

Hostel Facilities

The university provides excellent hostel facilities for its students. Separate hostels for men and women are available on all campuses, with well-furnished rooms, modern amenities, and a safe environment. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, recreation rooms, and facilities for indoor games. The hostel mess serves nutritious and hygienic food, catering to diverse dietary preferences. Wardens and security personnel ensure the safety and well-being of students.

Extracurricular Activities

The university encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities for their holistic development. Each campus has sports facilities for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and indoor games. The annual inter-college festival "Balaji Utsav" showcases the talents of students in cultural, literary, and sports events. The university also has various student clubs and societies focusing on different interests such as music, dance, drama, photography, and social service.

Community Outreach

The university is actively involved in community outreach programs. Students and faculty regularly organize health camps, awareness programs, and medical check-ups in rural and underserved areas. These activities not only provide healthcare services to the needy but also give students valuable hands-on experience and instill in them a sense of social responsibility.

Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth Placements

Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth Deemed University has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in their career development. While most medical graduates pursue higher studies or join hospitals, the university provides guidance and support for those seeking placements in healthcare organizations, research institutions, and industry.

Placements: Most students go for higher studies after MBBS. The university provides good guidance for those who want to pursue postgraduate studies in India or abroad.

Value: Compared to other deemed universities, Sri Balaji Vidyapeeth offers good value for money. The fees are reasonable considering the quality of education and facilities provided.
