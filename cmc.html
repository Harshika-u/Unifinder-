Basic Information

Christian Medical College, Vellore (CMC Vellore) is a private, minority-run medical college and hospital in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. Founded in 1900 by American missionary Ida S. Scudder, CMC Vellore has grown from a single-bed clinic to one of India's most prestigious medical institutions. The college is managed by the Christian Medical College Vellore Association, a Christian organization. It is affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University for its medical courses and with Vellore Institute of Technology for other academic programs.

CMC Vellore operates two campuses - the main campus at Vellore town and a secondary campus at Bagayam. The institution includes a network of primary, secondary and tertiary care hospitals with around 2,800 beds. It has been consistently ranked among the top medical colleges in India by various ranking agencies, including NIRF, which ranked it second in the medical category in 2023.

History

The story of Christian Medical College, Vellore begins with Ida S. Scudder, an American missionary who witnessed the death of three women in one night due to lack of proper medical care during childbirth. This incident inspired her to become a doctor and return to India to serve women's healthcare needs. In 1900, she started a small clinic with just one bed in Vellore.

The institution grew steadily, and in 1902, a 40-bed hospital was built. In 1918, a medical school for women was established, which was later upgraded to a co-educational college in 1947. The college became affiliated with the Madras University and began offering the MBBS degree.

Over the years, CMC Vellore has pioneered several medical firsts in India, including the first open-heart surgery in 1961, the first kidney transplant in 1971, and the first bone marrow transplant in 1986. Today, it stands as one of India's most respected medical institutions, known for its commitment to medical education, research, and healthcare services, especially for the underprivileged.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

CMC Vellore offers several undergraduate programs in medicine, nursing, and allied health sciences. The most sought-after program is the MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery), which has an annual intake of 100 students. The college also offers BSc programs in Nursing, Medical Laboratory Technology, Radiography, Operation Theatre Technology, and several other allied health disciplines.

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) :

The MBBS program at CMC Vellore is a 5.5-year course including a one-year compulsory rotating internship. The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in medical sciences and extensive clinical exposure. The selection process includes a NEET UG score, followed by special tests and interviews conducted by the college.

📝 Admissions – CMC Vellore 2025

1. Appear for NEET UG 2025 (Eligibility Test)

  • Conducted by NTA (National Testing Agency)
  • Mandatory for admission to MBBS program at CMC Vellore
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Botany & Zoology)
  • Minimum qualifying percentile required: 50th percentile for General, 40th for SC/ST/OBC
1

2. Apply for CMC Vellore Admission

  • Register on the official CMC Vellore website (www.cmch-vellore.edu)
  • Fill the application form with NEET UG details
  • Pay the application fee (approximately INR 2,500 for General category)
  • Submit required documents and photographs
2

3. Appear for CMC Vellore Special Tests

  • Shortlisted candidates based on NEET UG scores are called for special tests
  • Tests include:
    • General Paper (Aptitude, General Knowledge, Current Affairs)
    • Specific Paper (Biology, Physics, Chemistry)
  • Tests are conducted at various centers across India
3

4. Attend Interview Process

  • Candidates who qualify in the special tests are called for interviews
  • Interview panel assesses:
    • Motivation for joining CMC Vellore
    • Understanding of the institution's mission and values
    • Communication skills and personality
  • Interviews are conducted at CMC Vellore campus
4

5. Merit List & Seat Allotment

  • Final merit list is prepared based on:
    • NEET UG score (50% weightage)
    • Special test scores (35% weightage)
    • Interview performance (15% weightage)
  • Seat allotment based on merit and category
  • Allotment letter sent to selected candidates
5

6. Complete Admission Formalities

  • Submit original documents:
    • NEET UG Admit Card & Scorecard
    • Class 10 & 12 Mark Sheets
    • Transfer Certificate & Conduct Certificate
    • Community Certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs
  • Pay the tuition and other fees
  • Complete medical examination
  • Attend orientation program
6

🏕️ Campus Life

Life at Christian Medical College, Vellore is a unique blend of rigorous academics, clinical training, and extracurricular activities. The institution provides a nurturing environment that encourages holistic development of students. With students from diverse backgrounds and cultures, CMC Vellore offers a vibrant campus life that fosters learning, growth, and camaraderie.

Hostel Life

CMC Vellore provides separate hostel facilities for male and female students. The hostels are well-equipped with modern amenities and create a home away from home for students. Each hostel has wardens who ensure the well-being of the residents. The hostel life at CMC Vellore is known for its community spirit and cultural exchange.

Hostel facilities include:

  • Spacious and well-ventilated rooms
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Common rooms with TV and recreational facilities
  • Study rooms and libraries
  • Prayer and meditation rooms
  • 24/7 security
  • Mess facilities with nutritious and hygienic food

Annual Events and Festivals

CMC Vellore organizes several annual events and festivals that are eagerly awaited by students. These events provide a platform for students to showcase their talents, celebrate diversity, and create memorable experiences. The most prominent events include:

Zephyrus: The annual cultural festival of CMC Vellore, featuring music, dance, drama, and various competitions. Students from various departments participate enthusiastically in this multi-day event.

Spectrum: An inter-collegiate festival that invites students from other institutions to participate in academic, cultural, and sports competitions.

Sports Meet: Annual sports competition where students showcase their athletic abilities and compete in various sports events.

College Day: A celebration of the institution's founding, featuring special programs, awards, and cultural performances.
