Basic Information

Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS), Pilani is a premier private deemed university located in Pilani, Rajasthan. Established in 1929 as Birla College by the prominent industrialist Ghanshyam Das Birla, it was later transformed into BITS in 1964. BITS Pilani is widely recognized as one of India's leading engineering and technology institutes, known for its rigorous academic programs, innovative teaching methods, and strong industry connections. The institute has been consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in India by various ranking agencies.

History

BITS Pilani traces its origins to 1929 when it was established as Birla College by the visionary industrialist Ghanshyam Das Birla. The college was initially affiliated to the University of Rajasthan. In 1964, with the support of the Birla family and under the guidance of Dr. G.D. Birla, the college was transformed into Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) and granted the status of a deemed university. Since then, BITS Pilani has grown into a premier institution of higher learning in engineering and technology. The institute has expanded its operations with campuses in Dubai (2000), Goa (2004), and Hyderabad (2008), making it a truly global institution.

Vision and Mission

BITS Pilani is renowned for its research programs across various engineering disciplines. The institute offers Ph.D. programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

üèï Campus Life

BITS Pilani campus is spread over 990 acres in the semi-arid region of Rajasthan. The campus provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its modern infrastructure and well-planned layout, BITS Pilani offers a unique academic experience in one of India's premier engineering institutes.

Hostels and Accommodation

BITS Pilani provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The university has 11 hostels for boys and 1 hostel complex for girls. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at BITS Pilani is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

BITS Pilani encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at BITS Pilani is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Oasis," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The technical festival, "Apogee," attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills.

Facilities and Amenities

BITS Pilani provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at BITS Pilani is one of the largest technical libraries in India with over 2.5 lakh volumes.
