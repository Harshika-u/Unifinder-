Basic Information

FLAME University is a private university established in 2015 by the Foundation for Liberal and Management Education (FLAME) in Pune, Maharashtra. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is known for its innovative approach to liberal education in India. The university offers interdisciplinary programs that combine humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and professional education. FLAME University has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India for its unique educational model.

History

FLAME University was established in 2015 by the Foundation for Liberal and Management Education (FLAME), a non-profit organization. The university was founded with the vision of creating an institution that would provide a holistic and interdisciplinary education to students in India. FLAME started as a liberal arts college in 2004 and later evolved into a full-fledged university. The university's campus is located in Lavale, Pune, and is designed to provide a conducive environment for learning and personal growth. Over the years, FLAME University has emerged as a pioneer of liberal education in India.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a globally recognized institution of higher learning that promotes interdisciplinary education and research.
  • To create responsible and ethical leaders who can contribute to society in meaningful ways.
  • To foster critical thinking, creativity, and innovation among students.

Objectives

  • To provide a holistic education that integrates humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and professional education.
  • To develop students' intellectual, emotional, and social capacities through experiential learning.
  • To promote research and innovation that addresses contemporary challenges.
  • To create a diverse and inclusive community of learners and scholars.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

FLAME University is committed to promoting research that addresses contemporary challenges. The university offers Ph.D. programs in various disciplines, providing students with the opportunity to work on innovative research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

Ph.D. Programs:

The Ph.D. program at FLAME University is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to knowledge creation in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Humanities: Literature, Philosophy, Cultural Studies
  • Social Sciences: Psychology, Sociology, Economics, Public Policy
  • Management: Business Administration, Organizational Behavior
  • Sciences: Environmental Studies, Data Science
  • Interdisciplinary: Liberal Education, Experiential Learning

Admission to the Ph.D. program is based on the FLAME Research Aptitude Test followed by interviews. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support Ph.D. students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

FLAME University is located on a beautiful 60-acre campus in Lavale, Pune. The campus is designed to provide a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and staff. With its modern architecture and lush greenery, FLAME offers a unique academic experience in one of India's most vibrant cities.

Hostels and Accommodation

FLAME University provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The university has separate hostels for men and women, with comfortable rooms and common areas. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at FLAME is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

FLAME University encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports ground, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, and indoor games facilities. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year. Students participate in various sports competitions at the inter-university level.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at FLAME University is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and committees that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Oorja," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

FLAME University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities. The campus has well-equipped classrooms, seminar halls, and research centers. The university has a central library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at FLAME is one of the best liberal arts libraries in India with over 50,000 volumes.
