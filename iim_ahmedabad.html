Basic Information

The Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad (IIM-A) is a premier business school located in Ahmedabad, Gujarat, India. Established in 1961, the institute offers various postgraduate and doctoral programmes in management. It is widely recognized as one of the leading business schools in India and the world, consistently ranked as the #1 management institute in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). IIM-A was conceived by Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, a renowned scientist and industrialist Kasturbhai Lalbhai, with active support from the Government of India, Government of Gujarat, Harvard Business School, and the Ford Foundation. It was the second IIM to be established in India after IIM Calcutta. The institute is known for its rigorous academic curriculum, distinguished faculty, and strong industry connections. Its iconic campus, designed by renowned American architect Louis Kahn, is considered an architectural masterpiece.

History

The Indian Institute of Management Ahmedabad was established in 1961 with the active support of the Government of India, Government of Gujarat, Harvard Business School, and the Ford Foundation. It was the second IIM to be established in India after IIM Calcutta. The institute was conceived by Dr. Vikram Sarabhai, a renowned scientist, and Kasturbhai Lalbhai, an industrialist. They recognized the need for a world-class management education institution in India to support the country's industrial and economic development. The first batch of students was admitted in 1964. The institute's campus, designed by renowned American architect Louis Kahn, was completed in 1974 and is considered an architectural masterpiece. The campus design features exposed brickwork and geometric patterns, creating a unique blend of modernism and traditional Indian architecture. Over the years, IIM-A has grown in stature and reputation, becoming one of the most sought-after business schools in India and the world. It has consistently been ranked as the top management institute in India by various national and international rankings.

🎓 Courses Offered

Post Graduate Programme (PGP)

The Post Graduate Programme (PGP) is the flagship two-year, full-time residential program of IIM Ahmedabad. It is equivalent to an MBA and is designed to develop professional managers with strong conceptual foundations and analytical skills. The program offers a rigorous curriculum that combines classroom learning with practical exposure through case studies, projects, and internships.

Key Features of PGP:

  • Duration: 2 years (6 terms)
  • Intake: Approximately 385 students per batch
  • Curriculum: Core courses in the first year, elective courses in the second year
  • Pedagogy: Case-based teaching, group projects, presentations, and simulations
  • Summer Internship: 8-10 week internship at the end of the first year
  • International Exchange Program: Opportunity to study at partner B-schools abroad

The PGP program at IIM-A is highly competitive, with an acceptance rate of less than 0.5%. Graduates of the program are highly sought after by leading companies in India and abroad, with many going on to assume leadership positions in various industries.

Other Programmes

Besides the flagship PGP, IIM Ahmedabad offers several other programs catering to different needs and profiles of students:

Post Graduate Programme in Agri-Business Management (PGP-ABM)

  • A specialized two-year program focused on the agriculture and food business sector
  • Designed to develop managers and leaders for the agri-business sector
  • Combines general management education with specialized knowledge of agri-business
  • Intake: Approximately 45 students per batch

Post Graduate Programme in Management for Executives (PGPX)

  • A one-year full-time residential program for experienced professionals
  • Designed for mid-career executives with at least 4 years of work experience
  • Focuses on leadership development and strategic management
  • Intake: Approximately 120 students per batch

Fellow Programme in Management (FPM)

  • A doctoral program equivalent to a Ph.D. in management
  • Designed to develop researchers and academicians in management
  • Offers specialization in various functional areas of management
  • Duration: Typically 4-5 years

Executive Education Programmes

  • Faculty Development Programme (FDP): For management teachers and researchers
  • Customized Programs: Tailored programs for specific organizations

📝 Admissions – IIM Ahmedabad 2025

1. Appear for CAT (Common Admission Test)

  • Conducted annually by one of the IIMs on a rotational basis
  • Mandatory for admission to PGP and PGP-ABM programs
  • Tests Verbal Ability, Reading Comprehension, Data Interpretation, Logical Reasoning, and Quantitative Ability
  • Typically held in November
1

2. Achieve High Percentile in CAT

  • IIM-A requires a percentile of 99+ for general category candidates
  • Cutoff varies by category (General, OBC, SC, ST, EWS, PwD)
  • High CAT score is the first step in the selection process
2

3. Shortlisting for Written Test and Interview

  • Candidates are shortlisted based on CAT score, academic profile, and diversity factors
  • Shortlisting criteria includes:
    • CAT score (weightage: 70%)
    • Class 10 and 12 marks (weightage: 15% each)
    • Academic diversity and gender diversity factors
  • Approximately 1000 candidates are shortlisted for the next round
3

4. Analytical Writing Test (AWT) and Personal Interview (PI)

  • Shortlisted candidates appear for AWT and PI
  • AWT is a 30-minute written test to assess analytical and writing skills
  • PI assesses candidates on:
    • Communication skills
    • General awareness
    • Subject knowledge
    • Leadership potential
    • Personal attributes
4

5. Final Selection and Merit List

  • Final selection is based on:
    • CAT score (weightage: 35%)
    • AWT score (weightage: 10%)
    • PI score (weightage: 50%)
    • Class 10 marks (weightage: 2.5%)
    • Class 12 marks (weightage: 2.5%)
  • A merit list is prepared based on the composite score
  • Approximately 385 candidates are selected for the PGP program
5

6. Offer Acceptance and Admission

  • Selected candidates receive offer letters
  • Candidates need to accept the offer by paying the acceptance fee
  • Complete admission formalities including:
    • Submission of documents
    • Payment of first installment of fees
    • Medical fitness certificate
  • Academic session typically begins in June/July
6

🏕️ Campus Life

Life at IIM Ahmedabad is a unique blend of rigorous academics, vibrant extracurricular activities, and strong community bonding. The iconic campus, designed by renowned architect Louis Kahn, provides an inspiring environment for learning and personal growth. The red brick buildings with their geometric patterns and open spaces create a sense of harmony between nature and architecture. The campus is a self-contained ecosystem with academic buildings, student dormitories, faculty housing, sports facilities, and recreational spaces. The library, known as the Vikram Sarabhai Library, is one of the best management libraries in Asia with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Hostel Life

Hostel life is an integral part of the IIM-A experience. All students are provided accommodation on campus in dormitories that foster a sense of community and camaraderie. Each dormitory has its own unique culture and traditions, and students often form lifelong bonds with their dorm mates.

The dormitories are equipped with basic amenities including Wi-Fi, common rooms, and recreational facilities. The mess provides a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes. Late-night discussions, group study sessions, and impromptu celebrations are common occurrences in the dormitories.

The hostel experience at IIM-A is not just about accommodation but about learning to live with people from diverse backgrounds, developing interpersonal skills, and building a strong support network that lasts beyond the two years at the institute.

Festivals and Events

IIM Ahmedabad is known for its vibrant festivals and events that attract participants from across the country. These events provide opportunities for students to showcase their talents, network with industry leaders, and learn from experts.

Chaos

Chaos is the annual cultural festival of IIM-A, one of the largest college festivals in India. It features a variety of events including music, dance, dramatics, fashion shows, and art exhibitions. The festival attracts renowned artists and performers from across the country and provides a platform for students to express their creativity.

Confluence

Confluence is the annual management summit of IIM-A, bringing together industry leaders, policymakers, academicians, and students. It features panel discussions, workshops, competitions, and networking sessions. The summit provides insights into the latest trends and challenges in the business world.

Other Events

In addition to these major festivals, IIM-A hosts several other events throughout the year, including specialized summits, guest lectures, workshops, and sports tournaments. These events contribute to the holistic development of students and enhance their learning experience.

Sports and Recreation

Sports and recreation are an important part of campus life at IIM-A. The institute has excellent sports facilities including a gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, volleyball courts, and grounds for cricket and football. Students participate in various sports activities and tournaments throughout the year.

The annual sports festival "Sportech" attracts participants from various B-schools and colleges across the country. The institute also encourages students to participate in inter-IIM sports meets and other national-level competitions.

Besides sports, students engage in various recreational activities including yoga, meditation, trekking, and cultural pursuits. The campus provides ample opportunities for relaxation and rejuvenation amidst the rigorous academic schedule.
