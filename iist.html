Basic Information

The Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) is a deemed to be university and India's national institute for the study and development of space science. Located in Valiamala, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, it was established in 2007 by the Department of Space, Government of India. IIST is the first space institute in Asia and the first in the world to offer a complete range of undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral programs with specific focus on space science, technology and applications. The institute functions as an autonomous body under the Department of Space and is sponsored by the Government of India.

History

IIST was conceived with the vision to serve as a center of excellence in space science and technology education. The institute was inaugurated on September 14, 2007, by Dr. G. Madhavan Nair, then Chairman of ISRO. The first batch of undergraduate students was admitted in 2007. The institute started functioning from the campus of the Vikram Sarabhai Space Centre, Thiruvananthapuram. Later, it shifted to its own campus at Valiamala in 2010. Over the years, IIST has grown into a premier institution offering specialized programs in space science and technology, contributing significantly to India's space program.

🎓 Courses Offered

IIST offers a range of academic programs focused on space science and technology. The institute is known for its specialized curriculum designed to meet the requirements of the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and other space-related industries. The academic programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in science and engineering, with specific focus on space applications.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

IIST offers undergraduate programs in engineering with specialization in aerospace and avionics. The programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation in engineering principles with specific applications to space technology.

B.Tech Programs:

Program Duration Seats
B.Tech in Aerospace Engineering 4 Years 60
B.Tech in Avionics 4 Years 60
Dual Degree Program (B.Tech + M.S./M.Tech) 5 Years 20

Admission to the B.Tech programs is based on JEE Advanced scores. Candidates must have secured at least 75% aggregate marks in Class 12 (or equivalent) with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics as subjects. The Dual Degree program allows students to complete both B.Tech and M.S./M.Tech in five years with specialization in areas relevant to space technology.

POSTGRADUATE PROGRAMS

IIST offers a range of postgraduate programs in various disciplines of space science and technology. These programs are designed to provide advanced knowledge and research opportunities to students.

M.Tech Programs:

Program Duration Specializations
M.Tech 2 Years Aerodynamics and Flight Mechanics, Propulsion, Structures and Materials, Thermal and Propulsion
M.Tech 2 Years Control Systems, RF and Microwave Engineering, Digital Signal Processing, Power Electronics
M.Tech 2 Years Geoinformatics, Astronomy and Astrophysics, Earth System Science, Soft Computing and Machine Learning

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

IIST offers research programs leading to Ph.D. degree in various areas of space science and technology. The institute has a strong research focus with several research centers and laboratories dedicated to different aspects of space technology.

Ph.D. Programs:

The Ph.D. program at IIST is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

🏕 Campus Life

The Indian Institute of Space Science and Technology (IIST) campus is spread over 100 acres in the picturesque surroundings of Valiamala, Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, IIST offers a unique academic experience focused on space science and technology.

Hostels and Accommodation

IIST provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The institute has separate hostels for boys and girls with all necessary amenities. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at IIST is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

IIST encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, volleyball courts, badminton courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The institute also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at IIST is vibrant and diverse. The institute has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual technical festival, "Dhanak," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring technical competitions, workshops, and cultural performances. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

IIST provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The institute also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, and several cafeterias and food courts that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community.
