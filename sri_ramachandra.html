Basic Information

Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research (SRIHER) is a deemed to be university located in Porur, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 1985, the institution has grown into a comprehensive academic center offering programs in medicine, dentistry, nursing, allied health sciences, pharmacy, engineering, management, and other fields. SRIHER is known for its commitment to excellence in education, research, and healthcare services.

The institution is managed by the Sri Ramachandra Educational and Health Trust and is dedicated to the memory of Sri Ramachandra, a philanthropist who believed in providing quality education and healthcare to all sections of society. SRIHER has been accredited with the highest A++ grade by NAAC and has received recognition from various national and international bodies for its academic and research excellence.

History

Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research was established in 1985 by the Sri Ramachandra Educational and Health Trust. The institution was founded with the vision of providing quality education and healthcare services to the community. Initially, it started with a medical college and a hospital, and over the years, it has expanded to include various other academic and healthcare facilities.

In 1994, the institution was granted deemed to be university status by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. This status provided the institution with greater autonomy in academic, administrative, and financial matters, enabling it to develop innovative programs and research initiatives.

Over the years, SRIHER has grown into a comprehensive academic center with several constituent colleges and institutions offering programs in diverse fields. The institution has established collaborations with several international universities and research organizations, facilitating global exposure and research opportunities for students and faculty.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

SRIHER offers several undergraduate programs in medicine, dentistry, nursing, pharmacy, engineering, and other fields. The flagship program is the MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery), which has an annual intake of 250 students. The institution also offers BDS, BSc Nursing, BPharm, BTech, and various other undergraduate programs.

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) :

The MBBS program at SRIHER is a 5.5-year course including a one-year compulsory rotating internship. The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in medical sciences and extensive clinical exposure. Admission is based on the NEET UG score followed by counseling conducted by DGHS.
