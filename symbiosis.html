Basic Information

Symbiosis Institute of Business Management (SIBM) Pune is a premier B-school in India, established in 1978. It is the flagship institute of Symbiosis International (Deemed University). SIBM Pune has been consistently ranked among the top B-schools in India by various national and international ranking agencies. The institute is known for its academic excellence, industry interface, and global exposure. It offers a two-year full-time MBA program with various specializations, executive MBA programs, and PhD programs in management.

History

SIBM Pune was established in 1978 as a part of the Symbiosis group of educational institutions. Dr. S. B. Mujumdar laid the foundation of Symbiosis with the aim of promoting international understanding through quality education. The institute started with a small batch of students and has grown over the years to become one of the most sought-after B-schools in India. In 2002, SIBM Pune became a constituent of Symbiosis International (Deemed University). The institute moved to its new campus in Lavale, Pune in 2008. The campus is spread over 300 acres and provides state-of-the-art facilities for students. Over the years, SIBM Pune has expanded its academic offerings and has established itself as a center of excellence in management education.

🎓 Courses Offered

SIBM Pune offers a range of management programs designed to cater to the diverse needs of students and working professionals. The curriculum is regularly updated to align with industry requirements and global trends. The institute follows a trimester system and emphasizes experiential learning through case studies, projects, internships, and industry interactions.

MBA (Master of Business Administration)

The flagship two-year full-time MBA program is the most sought-after course at SIBM Pune. It offers specializations in various functional areas of management. The program is designed to develop business leaders with a global perspective and ethical values.

📝 Admissions – SIBM Pune 2025

1. Check Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks (45% for SC/ST)
  • Final year students can also apply
  • For Executive MBA: Minimum 3 years work experience
  • Valid score in SNAP exam
1

2. Register and Appear for SNAP

  • Register for SNAP exam at snaptest.org
  • SNAP is a computer-based test of 120 minutes
  • Sections: General English, Quantitative, Analytical & Logical Reasoning
  • SNAP score is valid for all Symbiosis B-schools
2

3. Apply to SIBM Pune

  • Register separately for SIBM Pune at sibmpune.edu.in
  • Pay application fee of INR 1000
  • Select your preferred specialization
  • Upload required documents and photographs
3

4. Shortlisting for GE-PIWAT

  • Shortlisting based on SNAP percentile
  • Cutoff varies by category (General, SC, ST, etc.)
  • Shortlisted candidates appear for GE-PIWAT:
    • GE: Group Exercise
    • PI: Personal Interview
    • WAT: Writing Ability Test
4

5. Attend GE-PIWAT Process

  • GE-PIWAT conducted at multiple centers across India
  • Process includes:
    • Group Exercise (case analysis, group discussion)
    • Personal Interview with faculty panel
    • Writing Ability Test (essay writing)
5

6. Merit List & Admission

  • Final merit list based on:
    • SNAP score (50% weightage)
    • GE-PIWAT performance (30% weightage)
    • Academic profile (20% weightage)
  • Selected candidates complete admission formalities
  • Pay first installment of fees
  • Submit documents for verification
6

🏕️ Campus Life

SIBM Pune is located in the Lavale Hill area, about 15 km from Pune city. The campus is spread over 300 acres and provides a serene environment conducive to learning and personal growth. The institute has state-of-the-art infrastructure with modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, a comprehensive library, and excellent sports facilities. The campus is Wi-Fi enabled and provides all necessary amenities for a comfortable stay.

Hostel Life

SIBM Pune provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls. The hostels are well-furnished with single occupancy rooms. Each room is equipped with a bed, study table, chair, and wardrobe. The hostels have common rooms with TV, indoor games facilities, and Wi-Fi connectivity. The mess serves nutritious and hygienic food with both vegetarian and non-vegetarian options. The hostel life at SIBM Pune is vibrant and fosters a sense of community among students.
