Basic Information

Banaras Hindu University (BHU) is a prestigious central university located in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. Founded in 1916 by Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya, BHU is one of the largest residential universities in Asia. The Faculty of Science at BHU is renowned for its excellence in teaching and research across various scientific disciplines. With state-of-the-art facilities and distinguished faculty, BHU has been consistently ranked among the top universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

Banaras Hindu University was established in 1916 by the great nationalist leader Pandit Madan Mohan Malaviya with the cooperation of great personalities like Dr. Annie Besant, who viewed it as the University of India. The foundation stone was laid by the then Viceroy of India, Lord Hardinge, on 4 February 1916. The Faculty of Science was one of the founding faculties of the university and has since been a center of excellence in scientific education and research. Over the years, BHU has produced numerous eminent scientists, academicians, and researchers who have made significant contributions to their respective fields.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

The Faculty of Science at BHU is renowned for its research programs across various disciplines. The institute offers PhD programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at BHU is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Physics: Condensed Matter Physics, Nuclear Physics, Electronics, Astrophysics
  • Chemistry: Organic Chemistry, Inorganic Chemistry, Physical Chemistry, Analytical Chemistry
  • Mathematics: Pure Mathematics, Applied Mathematics, Statistics
  • Botany: Plant Physiology, Genetics, Plant Pathology, Environmental Botany
  • Zoology: Cell Biology, Genetics, Endocrinology, Fisheries
  • Geology: Petrology, Mineralogy, Paleontology, Hydrogeology
  • Statistics: Probability, Statistical Inference, Operations Research, Biostatistics

Admission to the PhD program is based on the Research Entrance Test (RET) conducted by BHU, followed by interviews. The institute provides fellowships and research assistantships to support PhD students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

Banaras Hindu University campus is spread over 1300 acres of lush greenery in the holy city of Varanasi. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its blend of historic buildings and modern facilities, BHU offers a unique academic experience in one of India's oldest living cities.

Hostels and Accommodation

BHU provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has several hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and research students, separate for boys and girls. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at BHU is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

BHU encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at BHU is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Spandan," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

BHU provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at BHU is one of the largest university libraries in India with over 1.3 million volumes.
