Basic Information

The Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIM Calcutta or IIM-C) is a public business school located in Joka, Kolkata, West Bengal, India. Established in 1961, it was the first Indian Institute of Management. IIM Calcutta is one of only three triple-accredited business schools in India, along with IIM Bangalore and IIM Indore. It is the only business school in India with triple international accreditation: AACSB, AMBA and EQUIS. IIM Calcutta was declared an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India in 2017 through the IIM Act. The institute offers postgraduate, doctoral and executive education programs in management. It is known for its academic rigor, research excellence and strong industry connections.

History

The Indian Institute of Management Calcutta was established in November 1961 as the first national institute for postgraduate studies and research in management by the Government of India in collaboration with the Alfred P. Sloan School of Management (MIT Sloan), the Government of West Bengal, the Ford Foundation, and Indian industry. The institute was envisioned to be a center of excellence in management education, research, and consulting. The first batch of students joined in 1964, and the first director was Prof. K. T. Chandy. The institute initially functioned from a rented building on Alipore Road in Kolkata before moving to its present campus in Joka in 1975. Over the years, IIM Calcutta has grown into a premier institution known for its academic rigor, research output, and strong industry connections. It has played a pivotal role in shaping management education in India and has produced many business leaders, entrepreneurs, and academicians.

üéì Courses Offered

IIM Calcutta offers a range of academic programs in management education. These include postgraduate, doctoral, and executive education programs designed to cater to different segments of students and professionals. The institute is known for its rigorous curriculum, distinguished faculty, and emphasis on research and practical application of management concepts.

Post Graduate Program (PGP)

The two-year full-time Post Graduate Program (PGP) is the flagship program of IIM Calcutta. It is designed to develop skills, knowledge, and attitudes essential for careers in management. The program consists of six terms, with a summer internship between the first and second year. The curriculum includes core courses in the first year and elective courses in the second year, allowing students to specialize in their areas of interest.

The PGP at IIM Calcutta is consistently ranked among the top management programs in India and Asia. It has a strong focus on analytical thinking, leadership development, and ethical decision-making. The program attracts the best talent from across the country and prepares them for leadership roles in various sectors.

Post Graduate Program for Executives (PGPEX)

The one-year full-time Post Graduate Program for Executives (PGPEX) is designed for experienced professionals with substantial work experience. The program provides an opportunity for executives to enhance their managerial skills and accelerate their career growth. It combines rigorous academic coursework with practical insights from industry experts.

üèïÔ∏è Campus Life

The IIM Calcutta campus, spread over 135 acres in Joka, Kolkata, provides a vibrant and enriching environment for learning and personal growth. The campus features state-of-the-art academic buildings, modern hostels, sports facilities, and lush green spaces. The institute's architecture blends traditional elements with modern design, creating an inspiring atmosphere for students and faculty.

Hostel Life

IIM Calcutta provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The institute has several hostels, each with its unique character and culture. Hostel rooms are well-furnished and equipped with modern amenities. The hostels have common rooms, TV rooms, and recreational facilities where students can relax and socialize. The mess facilities offer a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes. Hostel life at IIM Calcutta is an integral part of the student experience, fostering lifelong friendships and networks.

Student Committees and Clubs

IIM Calcutta has a vibrant student community with numerous committees and clubs catering to diverse interests. These student-run organizations play a crucial role in the overall development of students by providing opportunities for leadership, teamwork, and extracurricular engagement. The committees handle various aspects of campus life, including academics, placements, alumni relations, cultural activities, and sports. The clubs focus on specific interests such as finance, marketing, consulting, entrepreneurship, arts, and social service.

Annual Festivals

IIM Calcutta hosts two major annual festivals that attract participants from across the country. Carpe Diem, the cultural festival, is a celebration of arts, music, dance, and literature. It features performances by renowned artists, competitions, workshops, and informal events. Intaglio, the international business summit, is one of the oldest and most prestigious B-school festivals in Asia. It brings together industry leaders, academicians, and students for knowledge sharing, competitions, and networking opportunities.

Sports and Recreation

IIM Calcutta offers excellent sports and recreational facilities to promote physical fitness and overall well-being among students. The campus has facilities for various indoor and outdoor sports, including cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, tennis, badminton, and table tennis. The institute also has a well-equipped gymnasium and a swimming pool. Regular tournaments and inter-hostel competitions are organized to encourage participation and sportsmanship. The annual sports meet is a major event that brings the entire campus together in a celebration of athleticism and team spirit.
