Basic Information

Krea University is India's first interdisciplinary university established in 2018. Located in Sri City, Andhra Pradesh, it offers a unique educational model that combines arts, sciences, and humanities with skill development. The university is known for its distinctive approach to education that emphasizes critical thinking, creativity, and ethical reasoning.

History

Krea University was established in 2018 by a group of eminent academicians, industry leaders, and philanthropists. The university was founded with the vision to create a new model of higher education in India that breaks down traditional disciplinary boundaries. The name "Krea" is derived from the Latin word "creare" meaning "to create", reflecting the university's focus on creativity and innovation.

Vision and Mission

  • To create a new model of higher education that is interdisciplinary, immersive, and impactful.
  • To nurture ethical leaders who can address complex global challenges through creative and critical thinking.
  • To foster a culture of inquiry, innovation, and inclusivity.
  • To bridge the gap between academia and industry through experiential learning.
  • To contribute to society through research and outreach programs.

📝 Admissions – Krea University 2025

1. Check Eligibility Criteria

  • 10+2 or equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks (55% for SC/ST/PwD)
  • Final year students can also apply
  • No upper age limit
1

2. Fill Krea Application Form

  • Visit apply.krea.edu.in
  • Register with valid email ID and mobile number
  • Fill personal, academic, and program details
  • Upload required documents (photograph, signature, etc.)
  • Pay application fee (INR 1500 for General, INR 750 for SC/ST/PwD)
2

3. Appear for Krea Aptitude Test (KAT)

  • KAT is a computer-based test
  • Syllabus: Quantitative Ability, Logical Reasoning, English Language, General Awareness
  • Exam duration: 120 minutes
  • Total questions: 100 (MCQ type)
  • No negative marking
3

4. Submit Statement of Purpose (SOP)

  • Write a 500-word SOP explaining your interest in the chosen program
  • Highlight your academic achievements, extracurricular activities, and career goals
  • Submit SOP along with the application form
  • SOP is an important component of the selection process
4

5. Personal Interview

  • Shortlisted candidates called for personal interview
  • Interview conducted by faculty members
  • Assesses communication skills, subject knowledge, and suitability for the program
  • Interviews may be conducted online or offline
5

6. Final Selection & Admission

  • Final merit list based on KAT score, SOP, and interview performance
  • Selected candidates receive offer letters
  • Complete admission formalities by paying fees
  • Submit original documents for verification
  • Report to campus on the specified date
6

🏕️ Campus Life

Life at Krea University is designed to be a transformative experience that goes beyond academics. The campus provides a vibrant environment where students can explore their interests, develop new skills, and build lifelong friendships.

Campus Facilities

Krea University's campus in Sri City is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities:

  • Academic Buildings: Modern classrooms, seminar halls, and laboratories with latest technology
  • Library: Well-stocked library with physical and digital resources, study spaces, and collaborative areas
  • Laboratories: Specialized labs for sciences, computer science, and creative arts
  • Sports Facilities: Gymnasium, sports fields, courts for basketball, volleyball, and badminton
  • Art Studios: Spaces for visual arts, performing arts, and music
  • Cafeteria: Multi-cuisine cafeteria serving nutritious and hygienic food
  • Medical Center: On-campus medical facilities with qualified staff

Hostel Life

Krea University provides comfortable and secure hostel facilities for students. The hostels are designed to create a home away from home with all necessary amenities:

  • Separate hostels for male and female students
  • Well-furnished rooms with attached bathrooms
  • 24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Common rooms with TV, newspapers, and recreational facilities
  • Laundry services
  • 24/7 security and medical facilities
  • Mess facilities providing nutritious and hygienic food
  • Housekeeping services

Student Activities

Krea University encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities to develop their talents and leadership skills:

  • Student Clubs: Various clubs for arts, culture, sports, debate, literature, and social service
  • Cultural Events: Annual cultural festival, talent shows, and performances
  • Sports Events: Inter-university tournaments, sports meets, and fitness programs
  • Workshops and Seminars: Regular sessions by industry experts and academicians
  • Community Service: Opportunities to engage with local communities through outreach programs
  • Entrepreneurship Cell: Platform for students to develop entrepreneurial skills and startup ideas

Annual Festival: Krea Utsav

Krea Utsav is the annual cultural and sports festival of Krea University. It is a celebration of creativity, talent, and sportsmanship. The festival includes:

  • Cultural performances by students and professional artists
  • Competitions in music, dance, drama, and fine arts
  • Sports tournaments and fitness challenges
  • Lectures and panel discussions by eminent personalities
  • Film screenings and exhibitions
  • Food stalls representing different cuisines
  • Entrepreneurship showcase and startup pitches
