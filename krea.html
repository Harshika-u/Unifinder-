Basic Information

Krea University is a private liberal arts university located in Sri City, Andhra Pradesh. Established in 2018, Krea offers a unique educational model focused on interwoven learning, which integrates arts and sciences with ethics, design thinking, and communication skills. The university aims to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving abilities in its students through an innovative curriculum that combines classroom learning with real-world application.

History

Krea University was established in 2018 by a group of eminent educationists, industry leaders, and intellectuals who envisioned a new model of higher education in India. The university was founded with the belief that education should be interdisciplinary, experiential, and focused on developing ethical leaders. Since its inception, Krea has been committed to creating an environment that encourages intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and creativity. The university has quickly gained recognition for its innovative approach to education and its focus on holistic development.

Vision and Mission

  • To create a unique educational model that develops ethical leaders with critical thinking and creative abilities.
  • To foster an environment of interwoven learning that integrates arts and sciences with real-world application.
  • To nurture a community of learners who are intellectually curious, socially responsible, and globally aware.

Objectives

  • To provide an interdisciplinary education that breaks down traditional barriers between disciplines.
  • To develop students' abilities to think critically, communicate effectively, and solve complex problems.
  • To create opportunities for experiential learning through internships, research, and community engagement.
  • To build a diverse and inclusive community that fosters collaboration and innovation.

🏕 Campus Life

Krea University's campus is spread over 40 acres in Sri City, Andhra Pradesh. The campus provides a modern and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its state-of-the-art facilities and sustainable architecture, Krea offers a unique academic experience designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and critical thinking.

Hostels and Accommodation

Krea University provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The university has modern, fully furnished hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and research students, separate for boys and girls. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at Krea is designed to be a home away from home, fostering a sense of community and collaboration among students from diverse backgrounds.

Sports and Recreation

Krea University encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports fields for cricket and football, courts for basketball and tennis, and facilities for indoor games. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Krea University is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

Krea University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, cafeteria, and transportation services. The library at Krea is designed to be a hub of knowledge and learning, with access to digital resources and study spaces that encourage collaborative learning.
