Basic Information

The Institute of Medical Sciences (IMS) is a premier medical institute under Banaras Hindu University (BHU) in Varanasi, India. Established in 1960, IMS BHU is one of the oldest and most prestigious medical institutions in the country. It is located within the sprawling BHU campus, which is one of the largest residential universities in Asia. The institute is recognized as an Institute of National Importance and has consistently been ranked among the top medical colleges in India by various ranking agencies.

IMS BHU offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various medical and health sciences disciplines. The institute is known for its academic excellence, research contributions, and quality healthcare services. It has a well-equipped hospital, Sir Sunderlal Hospital, which is a tertiary care center providing affordable healthcare to a large population in Eastern Uttar Pradesh and neighboring states.

History

The Institute of Medical Sciences, BHU, traces its roots to the Banaras Hindu University established by Pandit Madan Mohan Malviya in 1916. The medical education at BHU began with the establishment of the Faculty of Medicine in the same year. However, the Institute of Medical Sciences as a distinct entity was formally established in 1960 to provide specialized medical education and research facilities.

The foundation stone of the present medical college building was laid in 1956 by the then President of India, Dr. Rajendra Prasad. The institute was designed to be a center of excellence in medical education and healthcare. Over the years, IMS BHU has grown into a comprehensive medical institution with various departments and super-specialty centers.

The institute has been at the forefront of medical education and research in India. It has produced numerous eminent medical professionals who have made significant contributions to healthcare both in India and abroad. The institute has also been actively involved in community healthcare programs and medical research.

The Institute of Medical Sciences, BHU is located within the main campus of Banaras Hindu University, which is one of the largest residential universities in Asia. The lush green surroundings, architectural heritage, and modern facilities create an ideal atmosphere for medical education and research.

Hostel Life

The university provides excellent hostel facilities for students of the Institute of Medical Sciences. There are separate hostels for boys and girls with well-furnished rooms, mess facilities, and round-the-clock security. The hostels create a home away from home and foster a sense of community among students. Common rooms, TV rooms, and recreational areas are provided for relaxation and social interaction. The mess serves nutritious and hygienic food catering to diverse tastes. Wardens and support staff ensure the well-being of students and maintain discipline.

Sports and Recreation

BHU has excellent sports facilities that are available to all students, including those from the Institute of Medical Sciences. The campus has grounds for cricket, football, hockey, basketball, volleyball, and athletics. Indoor facilities for badminton, table tennis, chess, and carrom are also available. The university organizes inter-collegiate and inter-university tournaments, encouraging students to participate and excel in sports. Regular fitness sessions, yoga classes, and adventure activities are conducted to promote overall well-being.

Cultural Activities

BHU has a rich cultural heritage and organizes various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival "Spandan" is a major attraction that brings together students from all colleges under the university. It features music, dance, drama, art exhibitions, literary events, and fashion shows. Various clubs and societies dedicated to music, dance, drama, photography, and fine arts provide platforms for students to showcase their talents. Cultural exchange programs and workshops by renowned artists are regularly organized to enrich the cultural experience of students.

Student Clubs and Organizations

The Institute of Medical Sciences has various student clubs and organizations that cater to diverse interests. These include medical societies, debate clubs, environmental clubs, social service groups, and research forums. These clubs provide opportunities for students to pursue their passions, develop leadership skills, and engage with like-minded peers. Regular events, workshops, and competitions organized by these clubs enhance the overall learning experience and foster personal growth.

IMS BHU Placements and Career Opportunities
