Basic Information

Central University of Kerala (CUK) is a premier central university located in Kasaragod, the northernmost district of Kerala. Established in 2009 by the Government of India through an Act of Parliament, the university has rapidly emerged as a center of excellence in higher education. The university is committed to providing quality education, promoting research, and fostering innovation. With its focus on interdisciplinary studies and social inclusion, CUK aims to create a vibrant academic community that contributes to national development.

History

The Central University of Kerala was established in 2009 as one of the 15 new central universities envisioned by the Government of India. The university started functioning from a transit campus at Padennakkad, near Nileshwar, with Dr. G. Gopakumar as its first Vice-Chancellor. In 2019, the university shifted to its permanent campus at Periya, Kasaragod, spread across 310 acres of lush greenery. Over the years, the university has grown from a small institution with a few courses to a comprehensive university offering diverse programs across various disciplines. CUK has made significant strides in research, innovation, and community engagement, establishing itself as a prominent center of higher learning in the region.

Vision and Mission

üèïÔ∏è Campus Life

Central University of Kerala is located in the serene surroundings of Periya, Kasaragod district. The sprawling 310-acre campus provides an ideal environment for learning, research, and personal growth. With its lush greenery, modern infrastructure, and tranquil atmosphere, the campus offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and academic excellence. The university campus is designed to be eco-friendly with sustainable features like rainwater harvesting, solar power generation, and waste management systems. The campus is well-connected to nearby towns and cities, providing easy access to urban amenities while maintaining a peaceful academic environment.

Hostel Life

Central University of Kerala provides excellent hostel facilities for its students. Separate hostels for men and women are available on campus, with well-furnished rooms, modern amenities, and a safe environment. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, recreation rooms, and facilities for indoor and outdoor games. The hostel mess serves nutritious and hygienic food, catering to diverse dietary preferences. The hostel life at CUK fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among students, creating lifelong friendships and professional networks. The university also has guest houses for visiting faculty and parents.

Extracurricular Activities

Central University of Kerala believes in the holistic development of its students. The campus offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and student clubs. The university has excellent sports facilities, including grounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and badminton. The annual university festival "Samanvay" is a much-awaited event that showcases the talents of students in various fields. The university also encourages students to participate in community outreach programs and social service activities, instilling in them a sense of social responsibility.

Student Support Services

Central University of Kerala provides comprehensive student support services to ensure the well-being of its students. The university has a dedicated counseling center that offers psychological support and guidance to students. The campus also has a health center that provides medical care to students. The university library is well-stocked with books, journals, and digital resources. The e-library facility provides access to online databases and journals. The university also offers career guidance and placement services to help students plan their career paths. The university has a Women's Cell and a SC/ST Cell to address the specific needs of these student groups.
