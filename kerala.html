Basic Information

Central University of Kerala (CUK) is a premier central university located in Kasaragod, the northernmost district of Kerala. Established in 2009 by an Act of Parliament (No. 25 of 2009), the university has emerged as a center of excellence in higher education in the region. With its commitment to inclusive education and research, CUK aims to foster knowledge creation and dissemination in various disciplines while promoting social justice and national integration.

History

Central University of Kerala was established in 2009 as one of the 15 new central universities established during the XI Five Year Plan. The university started functioning from its transit campus at Padannakkad, Nileshwar, and Vidyanagar, Periye in Kasaragod district. The permanent campus is being developed at Tejaswini Hills, Periye, spread over 310 acres of lush greenery. Since its inception, the university has grown steadily, adding new academic programs and research centers while maintaining its commitment to quality education and social inclusivity.

Vision and Mission

  • To become a globally recognized center of excellence in teaching, research, and outreach.
  • To provide quality education that promotes intellectual growth, critical thinking, and creativity.
  • To foster an inclusive environment that values diversity and promotes social justice.

Objectives

  • To provide access to higher education for students from marginalized communities.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and innovation.
  • To develop socially responsible citizens who can contribute to national development.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions for academic excellence.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

Central University of Kerala is committed to promoting research and innovation across disciplines. The university offers PhD programs in various departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at CUK is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Humanities: English, Economics, History, Sociology, Political Science
  • Social Sciences: Social Work, Education
  • Natural Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Botany, Zoology
  • Environmental Science and related interdisciplinary areas

Admission to the PhD program is based on the Central University Common Entrance Test (CUCET) followed by interviews. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support PhD students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

Central University of Kerala operates from two campuses - the transit campuses at Padannakkad, Nileshwar, and Vidyanagar, Periye, and the permanent campus at Tejaswini Hills, Periye. The campuses are nestled in the lush greenery of Kasaragod district, providing a serene and conducive environment for learning and research. The university is committed to creating an inclusive campus community that celebrates diversity and promotes holistic development.

Hostels and Accommodation

Central University of Kerala provides hostel facilities to both male and female students. The university has several hostels with well-furnished rooms, modern amenities, and a conducive environment for study. Each hostel has a warden, resident tutors, and round-the-clock security. The hostels have common rooms, recreation facilities, and mess services that provide nutritious and hygienic food.

Sports and Recreation

The university encourages students to participate in sports and recreational activities for their physical and mental well-being. The campuses have facilities for various indoor and outdoor games including volleyball, basketball, badminton, cricket, and football. The university organizes annual sports meets and encourages students to participate in inter-university competitions.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Central University of Kerala is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Utsav," is a major event that showcases the diverse talents of students and provides a platform for cultural exchange. The university also celebrates national festivals and important days with enthusiasm.

Facilities and Amenities

The university provides modern facilities to support academic and research activities. The campuses have well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and computer centers. The central library has a vast collection of books, journals, and e-resources. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, cafeteria, and transportation services for the convenience of students and staff.
