Basic Information

Jawaharlal Nehru Centre for Advanced Scientific Research (JNCASR) is a multidisciplinary research institute located in Bangalore, India. It was established in 1989 by the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, with Prof. C.N.R. Rao as its founder president. The centre is dedicated to scientific research at the frontiers of knowledge and aims to be a world-class institution for advanced research in science and engineering. JNCASR was granted the status of Institution of Eminence by the Government of India in recognition of its contributions to scientific research.

History

JNCASR was established in 1989 with the vision of creating a world-class institution for scientific research in India. The centre was founded by Prof. C.N.R. Rao, a renowned scientist and Bharat Ratna awardee, who served as its President for many years. The institution started with a few research units and has grown over the years to become a premier research institution in the country. In 2018, JNCASR was granted the status of Institution of Eminence by the Government of India, which has provided it with greater autonomy and resources to enhance its research capabilities and global standing.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a world-class scientific institution for advanced research in science and engineering.
  • To pursue research at the frontiers of science and engineering.
  • To create knowledge and develop innovative technologies for the benefit of society.
  • To train young scientists in cutting-edge research.

JNCASR offers a range of academic programs focused on advanced research in various scientific disciplines. DOCTORAL PROGRAMS

JNCASR offers PhD programs in various disciplines of science and engineering. These programs are research-intensive and provide students with the opportunity to work on advanced research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

Admission to the PhD programs is based on national-level examinations such as JAM, GATE, CSIR-UGC NET, or JNCASR's own entrance test, followed by interviews. Students receive fellowships and research support throughout their program.

SPECIAL PROGRAMS

JNCASR offers several special programs aimed at nurturing young scientific talent and providing research exposure to students at different levels of their academic journey.

Summer Research Fellowship Program:

This program provides an opportunity to bright students to work with faculty members at JNCASR during summer vacation. It is open to students pursuing BSc, MSc, or BTech degrees.

Project Oriented Chemical Education (POCE):

POCE is a program designed for undergraduate chemistry students to gain hands-on research experience and exposure to advanced topics in chemistry.

Project Oriented Biological Education (POBE):

POBE is a program designed for undergraduate biology students to gain hands-on research experience and exposure to advanced topics in life sciences.

These programs provide students with early exposure to research and help them develop a scientific temperament and research mindset.

üèï Campus Life

JNCASR is located on a 30-acre campus in Jakkur, Bangalore. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its modern facilities and green surroundings, JNCASR offers a unique environment for scientific research and learning.

Hostels and Accommodation

JNCASR provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The hostels are well-equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. Each hostel has its own mess that provides nutritious and hygienic food. The hostel life at JNCASR fosters a sense of community and provides opportunities for interaction with peers and faculty members.

Sports and Recreation

JNCASR encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities including a gymnasium, tennis courts, basketball courts, and a cricket ground. Regular sports tournaments and events are organized to promote physical fitness and team spirit among students.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at JNCASR is vibrant and diverse. The institution organizes various cultural events throughout the year, including annual cultural festivals, science exhibitions, and distinguished lecture series. These events provide a platform for students to showcase their talents and interact with peers and faculty members in an informal setting.

Facilities and Amenities

JNCASR provides world-class facilities to support advanced scientific research. The campus has state-of-the-art laboratories, advanced research centers, and a central library with a vast collection of books and journals. The institution also has a health center, banking facilities, and cafeterias that cater to the diverse needs of students and staff.

Career Opportunities

JNCASR has a strong track record of producing highly skilled scientists and researchers who are in demand across various sectors. Most graduates pursue research careers in academic institutions and research organizations. Others find opportunities in industries with strong R&D focus.
