Basic Information

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-disciplinary, research-intensive university accredited with 'A++' grade by NAAC. Founded by world-renowned humanitarian and spiritual leader Mata Amritanandamayi Devi (Amma), the university has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India. With six campuses spread across the country in Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Coimbatore, Kochi, Mysuru, and Faridabad, Amrita offers over 250 undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including Engineering, Medicine, Dentistry, Nursing, Pharmacy, Arts & Sciences, Management, Communication, Social Work, and Education.

History

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham was established in 1994 as the School of Engineering in Coimbatore by Mata Amritanandamayi Devi with the vision to provide value-based education and to nurture compassionate professionals. In 2003, it was conferred the status of a Deemed-to-be University under Section 3 of the UGC Act. Over the years, the university has expanded to include multiple campuses across India and has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution offering programs in diverse fields. The university has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India, a testament to its commitment to excellence in education and research.

Vision and Mission

  • To provide value-based education and mould the character of the younger generation through a synthesis of science and spirituality.
  • To promote research and development in cutting-edge technologies and emerging areas of global importance.
  • To extend the benefits of education and technology to all sections of society, especially the rural and underprivileged.

Objectives

  • To develop world-class professionals with a strong sense of social responsibility and ethical values.
  • To foster interdisciplinary research that addresses critical challenges facing humanity.
  • To create an environment that nurtures innovation, creativity, and entrepreneurship.
  • To collaborate with leading national and international institutions for academic and research excellence.

🏕 Campus Life

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has six campuses spread across India in Amritapuri, Bengaluru, Coimbatore, Kochi, Mysuru, and Faridabad. Each campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its blend of modern infrastructure and natural beauty, Amrita offers a unique academic experience that promotes holistic development.

Hostels and Accommodation

Amrita provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls with modern amenities. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at Amrita is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration. The university also provides accommodation for international students with special facilities to help them adjust to the new environment.

Sports and Recreation

Amrita encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campuses have excellent sports facilities, including stadiums, gymnasiums, swimming pools, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket grounds, and football grounds. The university also has dedicated spaces for indoor games like table tennis, badminton, and chess. Regular sports tournaments and inter-college competitions are organized to promote sportsmanship and physical fitness among students.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Amrita is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Anokha," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents. Other cultural events include traditional festivals celebrations, food festivals, and international cultural exchange programs.

Facilities and Amenities

Amrita provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campuses have well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, central libraries with vast collections of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has health centers, banking facilities, post offices, shopping complexes, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The libraries at Amrita are digitalized and provide access to numerous online resources and databases. The university also has dedicated centers for innovation and entrepreneurship, providing students with opportunities to develop their ideas into viable products and services.
