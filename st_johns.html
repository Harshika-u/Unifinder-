Basic Information

St. John's Medical College is a premier medical institution located in Bangalore, Karnataka. Established in 1963 by the Catholic Bishops' Conference of India, it is run by the St. John's National Academy of Health Sciences. The institution is dedicated to providing healthcare education and services to all sections of society, with a special focus on the underserved and marginalized communities. The college is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and is recognized by the National Medical Commission.

History

St. John's Medical College was established in 1963 by the Catholic Bishops' Conference of India. The college was founded with the vision of creating a center of excellence in medical education, research, and healthcare services. The institution is part of the St. John's National Academy of Health Sciences, which also includes the St. John's Medical College Hospital and the St. John's Research Institute. Over the years, the college has grown into one of the most prestigious medical institutions in India, known for its academic excellence, research contributions, and commitment to healthcare for all.

🎓 Courses Offered

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) :

The MBBS program at St. John's Medical College is a 5.5-year course including a one-year compulsory rotating internship. The program is divided into three phases:

Phase Duration (years) Training
First One Pre-Clinical

The college offers 150 seats for the MBBS program, with admissions based on NEET UG scores. The curriculum is designed to provide comprehensive medical education with emphasis on clinical skills and community health.

🏕️ Campus Life

St. John's Medical College is located in the heart of Bangalore, Karnataka's vibrant capital city. The sprawling 132-acre campus provides an ideal environment for learning, research, and personal growth. With its lush greenery, modern infrastructure, and serene atmosphere, the campus offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and academic excellence. The college is part of the St. John's National Academy of Health Sciences, which includes the St. John's Medical College Hospital and the St. John's Research Institute. This integrated campus provides students with unique opportunities for interdisciplinary learning and research.

Hostel Life

St. John's Medical College provides excellent hostel facilities for its students. Separate hostels for men and women are available on campus, with well-furnished rooms, modern amenities, and a safe environment. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, recreation rooms, and facilities for indoor games. The hostel mess serves nutritious and hygienic food, catering to diverse dietary preferences. The hostel life at St. John's fosters a sense of community and camaraderie among students, creating lifelong friendships and professional networks.

Extracurricular Activities

St. John's Medical College believes in the holistic development of its students. The campus offers a wide range of extracurricular activities, including sports, cultural events, and student clubs. The college has excellent sports facilities, including grounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and tennis. The annual college festival "Caduceus" is a much-awaited event that showcases the talents of students in various fields. The college also encourages students to participate in community outreach programs and health camps, instilling in them a sense of social responsibility.

Student Support Services

St. John's Medical College provides comprehensive student support services to ensure the well-being of its students. The college has a dedicated counseling center that offers psychological support and guidance to students. The campus also has a health center that provides medical care to students. The college library is well-stocked with medical textbooks, journals, and digital resources. The e-library facility provides access to online databases and journals. The college also offers career guidance and placement services to help students plan their career paths.

🎓 Student Reviews

Review 1: Excellent faculty and infrastructure

Academics: The faculty at St. John's is exceptional. Professors are not just knowledgeable but also approachable. They encourage interactive learning and provide excellent clinical exposure. The curriculum is comprehensive and updated regularly.
Infrastructure: The college has state-of-the-art facilities. The labs are well-equipped, the library is extensive, and the hospital attached to the college provides great hands-on experience.
Hostel Life: Hostels are comfortable with all necessary amenities. The mess food is decent and there are plenty of food options on campus.
Overall: St. John's provides a holistic learning environment that prepares you well for a career in medicine.

Review 2: Great clinical exposure and research opportunities

Clinical Exposure: The best part about St. John's is the clinical exposure we get right from the early years. The hospital sees a diverse patient population, which helps in understanding various diseases and conditions.
Research: There are ample opportunities for research. The college encourages students to participate in research projects and publish papers.
Extracurricular: The college festival "Caduceus" is amazing. There are also various clubs and societies you can join based on your interests.
Placements: Most students go for higher studies, but those who directly join hospitals get good packages. The college has a good reputation in the healthcare industry.

Review 3: A well-rounded medical education experience

Academics: The curriculum is rigorous but well-structured. The college emphasizes not just theoretical knowledge but also practical skills. The regular assessments help in staying on track.
Faculty: The professors are experts in their fields and are always willing to help. The mentorship program is particularly beneficial.
Campus Life: The campus is beautiful with plenty of green spaces. The sports facilities are excellent, and there are various cultural events throughout the year.
Community Outreach: The college's focus on community health is commendable. The rural health postings provide valuable insights into healthcare challenges in rural areas.

Review 4: Good value for money with excellent education

Value: Compared to other private medical colleges, St. John's offers good value for money. The fees are reasonable considering the quality of education and facilities provided.
Facilities: The labs, library, and lecture halls are well-maintained. The college hospital has all the modern equipment needed for training.
Location: Being in Bangalore is a big advantage. The city has great weather, plenty of opportunities for internships, and a vibrant student life.
Alumni Network: The alumni network is strong and supportive. They often come back for talks and mentorship sessions, which is very helpful for career guidance.
