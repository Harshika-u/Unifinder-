Basic Information

Shiv Nadar University (SNU) is a private interdisciplinary research university founded in 2011 by Shiv Nadar, founder of HCL. The university is located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India. It was established as part of the Shiv Nadar Foundation, a philanthropic initiative by the Nadar family. The university has been granted the status of 'State Private University' by the Government of Uttar Pradesh and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

SNU is known for its multidisciplinary approach to education, offering undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various fields including engineering, natural sciences, humanities and social sciences, management, and entrepreneurship. The university follows a credit-based system and emphasizes research and innovation from the undergraduate level.

History

Shiv Nadar University was established in 2011 by the Shiv Nadar Foundation with the vision to create a world-class, multidisciplinary university that would transform India's higher education landscape. The university was founded by Shiv Nadar, a pioneer in the Indian IT industry and founder of HCL Technologies.

The university began operations with the School of Engineering and has since expanded to include several other schools and departments. In 2013, the university established the School of Natural Sciences, followed by the School of Humanities and Social Sciences in 2014. The School of Management and Entrepreneurship was established in 2016.

In 2020, Shiv Nadar University was granted the status of 'Institution of Eminence' by the Government of India, recognizing its potential to become a world-class institution. The university has since focused on strengthening its research capabilities and expanding its academic offerings.

Over the years, SNU has developed collaborations with several international universities and research institutions, providing students and faculty with opportunities for global exposure and research partnerships. The university has also been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by various ranking agencies.

📝 Admissions – Shiv Nadar University

1. Application Process

  • Apply online through the official university website
  • Fill the application form with personal and academic details
  • Pay the application fee (approximately INR 1,200)
  • Submit required documents and photographs
1

2. Eligibility Criteria

  • For B.Tech: Minimum 60% in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics
  • For B.Sc: Minimum 50% in 10+2 with relevant subjects
  • For BA/BBA: Minimum 50% in 10+2 in any stream
  • For M.Tech/MBA: Bachelor's degree with minimum 60% marks
2

3. Entrance Examination

  • Appear for SNUSAT (Shiv Nadar University Scholastic Aptitude Test)
  • For B.Tech: JEE Main scores are also accepted
  • SAT/ACT scores are considered for international students
  • SNUSAT includes sections on quantitative ability, logical reasoning, and English proficiency
3

4. Interview Process

  • Shortlisted candidates are called for personal interviews
  • Interviews assess subject knowledge, communication skills, and aptitude
  • Some programs may have additional rounds like group discussions
  • For creative programs, portfolio review or aptitude tests may be conducted
4

5. Merit List and Admission Offer

  • Merit list is prepared based on entrance exam scores and interview performance
  • Selected candidates receive admission offers via email
  • Offer letter includes details about the program, fees, and deadline for acceptance
  • Waitlisted candidates may receive offers based on seat availability
5

6. Fee Payment and Document Verification

  • Pay the admission fee to confirm the seat
  • Submit original documents for verification:
    • Class 10 and 12 Mark Sheets
    • Entrance exam scorecard
    • Transfer Certificate
    • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
    • Category Certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs
  • Complete the enrollment process and attend orientation

🏕️ Campus Life

Shiv Nadar University offers a vibrant and diverse campus life that complements its rigorous academic programs. The 286-acre campus is designed to provide students with a holistic learning environment that fosters intellectual growth, personal development, and social engagement. The campus features state-of-the-art facilities, green spaces, and modern architecture that creates an inspiring atmosphere for learning and innovation.

Residential Life

Shiv Nadar University is a fully residential campus, providing on-campus accommodation to all its students. The hostels are designed to create a home away from home, offering comfortable living spaces and fostering a sense of community among students.

  • Separate hostels for male and female students
  • Rooms equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and study areas
  • Common rooms with recreational facilities
  • 24/7 security and medical assistance
  • Laundry services and housekeeping
  • Proximity to academic buildings and other campus facilities

The residential experience at SNU goes beyond just providing accommodation. It aims to create a nurturing environment where students can develop life skills, build lasting friendships, and engage in meaningful interactions with peers from diverse backgrounds.

Clubs and Societies

Shiv Nadar University encourages students to pursue their interests and passions beyond academics through a wide range of clubs and societies. These student-led organizations provide opportunities for leadership, creativity, and personal growth.

  • Technical Clubs: Robotics, Coding, Electronics, and Innovation clubs that foster technical skills and problem-solving abilities.
  • Cultural Clubs: Music, Dance, Drama, Art, and Photography clubs that celebrate artistic expression and cultural diversity.
  • Sports Clubs: Cricket, Football, Basketball, Tennis, Badminton, and Athletics clubs that promote physical fitness and team spirit.
  • Academic Clubs: Debating Society, Quiz Club, Science Club, and Literature Society that enhance intellectual engagement.
  • Social Impact Clubs: Community service, environmental awareness, and social entrepreneurship clubs that encourage civic engagement.

These clubs organize various events, workshops, and competitions throughout the year, providing students with opportunities to showcase their talents and develop new skills.

Annual Festivals and Events

Shiv Nadar University hosts several annual festivals and events that bring together students, faculty, and staff in celebration of creativity, innovation, and community spirit. These events are highlights of the academic calendar and eagerly anticipated by the entire university community.

  • Brouhaha: The annual cultural festival of SNU, featuring music, dance, drama, fashion shows, and art exhibitions. It attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their artistic talents.
  • Parivartan: The technical festival that celebrates innovation and technology. It includes hackathons, robotics competitions, technical workshops, and talks by industry experts.
  • Sports Meet: An annual sports tournament where students compete in various athletic events and team sports. It promotes physical fitness and sportsmanship among students.
  • International Day: A celebration of global diversity, featuring cultural performances, food festivals, and exhibitions representing different countries and cultures.
  • Research Conclave: An academic event that showcases research work by students and faculty, featuring presentations, panel discussions, and keynote lectures by eminent scholars.

These events not only provide entertainment and recreational opportunities but also help in developing organizational skills, teamwork, and leadership abilities among students.
