Basic Information

Shiv Nadar University (SNU) is a private interdisciplinary research university founded in 2011 by Shiv Nadar, founder of HCL. Located in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh, the university is spread across 286 acres of lush green campus. SNU was granted the status of 'Institute of Eminence' by the University Grants Commission (UGC) in 2020. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines including engineering, humanities, social sciences, natural sciences, and management. With a focus on research-driven education, SNU has established itself as one of India's leading private universities.

History

Shiv Nadar University was established in 2011 by the Shiv Nadar Foundation, a philanthropic initiative by Shiv Nadar, the founder of HCL Technologies. The university was envisioned as a world-class institution that would provide interdisciplinary education and promote research and innovation. In 2020, SNU was granted the prestigious status of 'Institute of Eminence' by the Government of India, recognizing its potential to compete globally. Over the years, the university has grown significantly in terms of student enrollment, faculty strength, research output, and infrastructure development. Today, SNU stands as a symbol of excellence in higher education in India, attracting students and faculty from across the country and abroad.

Vision and Mission

  • To become a globally recognized university known for its research excellence and interdisciplinary education.
  • To nurture ethical, innovative, and responsible leaders who will make a positive impact on society.
  • To create an environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and lifelong learning.

Objectives

  • To provide a transformative educational experience that combines academic rigor with practical application.
  • To promote cutting-edge research that addresses global challenges and contributes to knowledge creation.
  • To build a diverse and inclusive community of students, faculty, and staff from different backgrounds and cultures.
  • To establish collaborations with leading national and international institutions for academic and research excellence.

üèï Campus Life

Shiv Nadar University campus is spread over 286 acres of lush green landscape in Greater Noida. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure and modern facilities, SNU offers a world-class academic experience in the National Capital Region.

Hostels and Accommodation

SNU provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls, with options for single, double, and triple occupancy rooms. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at SNU is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

SNU encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at SNU is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Brouhaha," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

SNU provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at SNU is a modern facility with digital resources and collaborative learning spaces.
