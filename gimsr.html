About GITAM School of Medical Sciences

GITAM School of Medical Sciences is a constituent college of GITAM (Deemed to be University), established in 2015. Located in the beautiful city of Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, the institution is dedicated to providing quality medical education and healthcare services to the nation. The medical college is attached to a 750-bed teaching hospital that provides comprehensive healthcare services to the community and serves as a training ground for medical students.

Vision and Mission

Vision: To be a globally recognized institution of excellence in medical education, research, and healthcare services.

Mission: To produce competent, compassionate, and ethical healthcare professionals who will serve the community with dedication and contribute to the advancement of medical science through research and innovation.

Key Features

  • State-of-the-art infrastructure with modern laboratories and classrooms
  • 750-bed multi-specialty teaching hospital with advanced medical facilities
  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty members
  • Emphasis on research and innovation in medical sciences
  • Comprehensive curriculum with integrated teaching methodology
  • Focus on community healthcare and outreach programs
  • Modern hostel facilities with all amenities
  • Sports and extracurricular activities for overall development

üéì Courses Offered

GITAM School of Medical Sciences offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate medical programs designed to meet the evolving needs of the healthcare sector. The curriculum is designed to provide a strong foundation in medical sciences along with practical clinical training.

UNDERGRADUATE PROGRAMS

The undergraduate programs at GITAM School of Medical Sciences focus on building a strong foundation in medical sciences and developing clinical skills. The flagship program is the MBBS course, which is approved by the National Medical Commission (NMC).

Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery (MBBS) :

The MBBS program at GITAM School of Medical Sciences is a 5.5-year course including a one-year compulsory rotating internship. The program follows the curriculum prescribed by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and is designed to produce competent medical professionals.

Attribute Details
Duration 5.5 years (4.5 years academics + 1 year internship)
Intake Capacity 150 seats per year
Eligibility 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English
Admission Process Based on NEET UG score
Curriculum As per NMC guidelines with integrated teaching approach

The MBBS curriculum is divided into three phases:

  • Phase I (Pre-Clinical): Anatomy, Physiology, and Biochemistry
  • Phase II (Para-Clinical): Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Forensic Medicine, and Community Medicine
  • Phase III (Clinical): Medicine and allied specialties, Surgery and allied specialties, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Pediatrics, and other clinical subjects

B.Sc Nursing Program

The B.Sc Nursing program at GITAM School of Medical Sciences is a 4-year undergraduate program designed to prepare students to become competent nursing professionals. The program combines theoretical knowledge with extensive practical training.

B.Sc Nursing Course Details:

Attribute Details
Duration 4 years
Intake Capacity 60 seats per year
Eligibility 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and English
Admission Process Based on merit in qualifying examination and entrance test
Curriculum As per Indian Nursing Council guidelines

üèïÔ∏è Campus Life

GITAM School of Medical Sciences offers a vibrant campus life that promotes academic excellence, personal growth, and holistic development. The campus is spread over 100 acres and provides state-of-the-art facilities for students to pursue their medical education in a conducive environment.

Infrastructure and Facilities

The campus boasts modern infrastructure with well-equipped lecture halls, laboratories, and libraries. The lecture halls are designed to facilitate interactive learning with audio-visual aids. The laboratories are equipped with the latest equipment and technology to provide hands-on training to students.

The central library houses an extensive collection of medical books, journals, and digital resources. It provides a quiet and conducive environment for study and research. The campus also has high-speed internet connectivity throughout, enabling students to access online resources easily.

Hostel Life

GITAM School of Medical Sciences provides comfortable and secure hostel facilities for students. The hostels are separate for boys and girls and offer various accommodation options including AC and non-AC rooms with single or double occupancy. Each hostel has common rooms, recreation areas, and round-the-clock security.

The hostel mess provides nutritious and hygienic food catering to diverse tastes. The campus also has cafeterias and food courts that offer a variety of cuisines. The hostel environment promotes camaraderie among students and provides a home away from home.

Sports and Extracurricular Activities

The campus encourages students to participate in sports and extracurricular activities for their overall development. It has facilities for various indoor and outdoor games including cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, badminton, and table tennis. The college organizes annual sports meets and tournaments to promote sportsmanship and physical fitness.

Cultural activities are an integral part of campus life at GITAM School of Medical Sciences. The college celebrates various festivals and organizes cultural events throughout the year. Students can join various clubs and societies based on their interests such as music, dance, drama, literature, and fine arts.

Healthcare and Wellness

The institute has a dedicated healthcare center on campus to provide medical care to students. Regular health check-ups are conducted, and counseling services are available for students' mental and emotional well-being. The campus also has a gymnasium and yoga center to promote physical fitness and stress management.

The attached teaching hospital provides students with opportunities for clinical training and exposure to real-life medical scenarios. Students can also participate in community health programs and outreach activities organized by the institute, helping them develop empathy and social responsibility.
