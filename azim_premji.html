Basic Information

Azim Premji University is a private university located in Bengaluru, Karnataka, India. Established in 2010 by the Azim Premji Foundation, the university is committed to developing outstanding education professionals with a deep sense of social commitment and ethical perspective. The university aims to contribute to the realization of a just, equitable, humane, and sustainable society through education.

The university offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields including education, development, and related disciplines. It has a unique approach to education that integrates theory with practice and emphasizes the development of critical thinking, ethical values, and social commitment among students.

History

Azim Premji University was established in 2010 by the Azim Premji Foundation, a not-for-profit organization founded by Azim Premji, chairman of Wipro Limited. The foundation has been working in the field of education for over a decade, focusing on improving the quality of education in India, particularly in rural areas.

The university was established with the vision of creating a center of excellence in education that would develop professionals who could contribute to social change. The university was granted recognition by the University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 22 of the UGC Act, 1956.

Since its inception, the university has grown steadily, adding new programs and expanding its infrastructure. It has established itself as a unique institution that integrates academic rigor with social commitment and ethical values. The university has also developed strong partnerships with various organizations working in the field of education and development.

1. Check Eligibility Criteria

  • For UG programs: 10+2 with minimum 50% marks
  • For PG programs: Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% marks
  • For Ph.D.: Master's degree with minimum 55% marks
  • Relevant work experience is preferred for PG and Ph.D. programs
  • Strong commitment to social change is valued
1

2. Register for Entrance Exam

  • Visit azimpremjiuniversity.edu.in/admissions
  • Create an account on the admission portal
  • Fill the application form with personal and academic details
  • Upload required documents and photographs
  • Pay the application fee (INR 500 for UG, INR 750 for PG/Ph.D.)
2

3. Appear for Entrance Test/Interview

  • For UG programs: National Entrance Test (NET)
  • For PG programs: Written test followed by personal interview
  • For Ph.D.: Research proposal presentation and interview
  • Test pattern varies by program (MCQs, descriptive, etc.)
  • Tests assess subject knowledge, analytical skills, and social commitment
3

4. Check Results & Merit List

  • Results are published on the university website
  • Merit list is prepared based on entrance test/interview performance
  • Separate merit lists for different categories (General, SC/ST, OBC, EWS)
  • Cutoff scores vary by program and category
4

5. Counseling & Seat Allotment

  • Qualified candidates are called for counseling
  • Document verification process
  • Choice filling for programs
  • Seat allotment based on merit and preferences
  • Payment of admission fee to confirm the seat

6. Final Admission & Reporting

    • 10th and 12th mark sheets and certificates

    • Transfer certificate

🏕️ Campus Life

Azim Premji University provides a vibrant and enriching campus life that complements the academic experience. The campus is spread over 80 acres in Bengaluru and provides a serene environment for learning and personal growth. The university believes in the holistic development of students and provides numerous opportunities for extracurricular activities, sports, cultural events, and community engagement.

Hostel Life

The university provides excellent hostel facilities for students. Separate hostels for boys and girls are available with well-furnished rooms, mess facilities, and round-the-clock security. The hostels create a home away from home and foster a sense of community among students. Common rooms, TV rooms, and recreational areas are provided for relaxation and social interaction. The mess serves nutritious and hygienic food catering to diverse tastes. Wardens and support staff ensure the well-being of students and maintain discipline.

Sports and Recreation

The university has excellent sports facilities that are available to all students. The campus has grounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and athletics. Indoor facilities for badminton, table tennis, chess, and carrom are also available. The university organizes inter-collegiate and inter-university tournaments, encouraging students to participate and excel in sports. Regular fitness sessions, yoga classes, and adventure activities are conducted to promote overall well-being.

Cultural Activities

The university celebrates cultural diversity through various events and festivals throughout the year. The annual cultural festival "Sambhav" is a major attraction that brings together students from all programs. It features music, dance, drama, art exhibitions, literary events, and fashion shows. Various clubs and societies dedicated to music, dance, drama, photography, and fine arts provide platforms for students to showcase their talents. Cultural exchange programs and workshops by renowned artists are regularly organized to enrich the cultural experience of students.

Student Clubs and Organizations

The university has a vibrant ecosystem of student clubs and organizations catering to diverse interests. These include debating societies, environmental clubs, social service groups, and entrepreneurship cells. These clubs provide opportunities for students to pursue their passions, develop leadership skills, and engage with like-minded peers. Regular events, workshops, and competitions organized by these clubs enhance the overall learning experience and foster personal growth.

Azim Premji University Placements

Azim Premji University has a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to ensure excellent career opportunities for its students.
