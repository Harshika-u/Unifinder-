Basic Information

Azim Premji University is a private university established in 2010 by the Azim Premji Foundation, a non-profit organization. The university is located in Bangalore, Karnataka, and is recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India. With a focus on education and development, the university aims to create professionals with deep social commitment and ethical leadership. The university has consistently been ranked among the top private universities in India for its innovative programs and social impact.

History

Azim Premji University was established in 2010 by the Azim Premji Foundation as a response to the critical challenges facing the education and development sectors in India. The university was founded with the vision of creating a just, equitable, humane, and sustainable society. It began with a small group of students and faculty members and has since grown into a full-fledged university offering various undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs. The university's approach to education is deeply rooted in the values of social justice, equity, and human dignity.

Vision and Mission

  • To create a just, equitable, humane, and sustainable society through education and development.
  • To develop outstanding professionals with deep social commitment and ethical leadership.
  • To promote knowledge creation and dissemination through research and practice.

Objectives

  • To provide quality education that integrates theory and practice.
  • To develop professionals who can contribute to social change and development.
  • To create knowledge through research that addresses critical social issues.
  • To collaborate with organizations working in education and development sectors.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

Azim Premji University is committed to promoting research that addresses critical social issues. The university offers Ph.D. programs in various disciplines, providing students with the opportunity to work on innovative research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

Ph.D. Programs:

The Ph.D. program at Azim Premji University is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to knowledge creation in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Education: Curriculum and Pedagogy, Teacher Education, Education Policy
  • Development: Rural Development, Urban Development, Livelihoods
  • Environment: Climate Change, Water Policy, Sustainable Development
  • Public Policy: Social Policy, Health Policy, Education Policy
  • Livelihoods: Agriculture, Rural Livelihoods, Urban Livelihoods
  • Social Work: Community Development, Social Work Education

Admission to the Ph.D. program is based on the Azim Premji University Research Entrance Test followed by interviews. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support Ph.D. students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

Azim Premji University is located on a beautiful 80-acre campus in Sarjapura, Bangalore. The campus is designed to be environmentally sustainable and provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and staff. With its modern facilities and lush greenery, the university offers a unique academic experience in India's Silicon Valley.

Hostels and Accommodation

Azim Premji University provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has separate hostels for men and women, with comfortable rooms and common areas. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at the university is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

The university encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, sports ground, and indoor games facilities. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year. Students participate in various sports competitions at the inter-university level.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Azim Premji University is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and committees that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Samaagam," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

Azim Premji University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped classrooms, seminar halls, and research centers. The university has a central library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources on education and development. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at the university is one of the best in the country with over 50,000 volumes.
