Basic Information

Gitam University, officially known as Gandhi Institute of Technology and Management, is a premier deemed university in India with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. Established in 1980, it was conferred the status of deemed to be university in 2007 by the University Grants Commission. Gitam is renowned for its engineering programs and has consistently been ranked among the top private engineering institutions in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF). The university is known for its industry-oriented curriculum, research facilities, and strong alumni network.

History

Gitam University was established in 1980 as an engineering college in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, by Dr. M.V.V.S. Murthi, a visionary educationist and philanthropist. The institute started with a modest beginning but soon gained recognition for its quality education and infrastructure. In 2007, Gitam was granted the status of deemed to be university by the UGC, enabling it to design its own curriculum and academic programs. Over the years, the university has expanded its reach with campuses in Hyderabad (2012) and Bengaluru (2019). Gitam has grown from a single engineering college to a comprehensive university offering programs in engineering, science, management, law, pharmacy, architecture, and humanities.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

Gitam University is renowned for its research programs across various engineering disciplines. The university offers PhD programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at Gitam University is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

🏕 Campus Life

Gitam University has three campuses located in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. The Visakhapatnam campus, spread over 100 acres, is the oldest and largest campus of the university. The campuses provide a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, Gitam University offers an exceptional academic experience in three major cities of India.

Hostels and Accommodation

Gitam University provides on-campus accommodation to a majority of its students. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls across all three campuses. Each hostel is equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, reading rooms, and recreational areas. The hostels have mess facilities that provide hygienic and nutritious food. The hostel life at Gitam is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

Gitam University encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campuses have excellent sports facilities, including stadiums, gymnasiums, swimming pools, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket grounds, and football fields. The university also has indoor sports complexes for badminton, table tennis, and volleyball. Regular sports tournaments and competitions are organized to promote physical fitness and team spirit among students.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Gitam University is vibrant and diverse. The university has numerous student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Samyak," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The technical festival, "Pragnya," provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and innovations. These festivals attract participants from colleges across the country and provide a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

Gitam University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campuses have well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, central libraries with vast collections of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has health centers, banking facilities, post offices, shopping complexes, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The central library at Gitam is one of the largest technical libraries in the region with access to numerous online journals and databases.
