Basic Information

The Indian Statistical Institute (ISI) is a premier research institution and deemed university dedicated to the research, teaching, and application of statistics, natural sciences, and social sciences. Founded in 1931 by Professor Prasanta Chandra Mahalanobis, ISI is recognized as an Institute of National Importance by the Government of India. The institute has its main campus in Kolkata, with additional centers in Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Tezpur. ISI has played a pivotal role in the development of statistical science in India and has contributed significantly to various national planning and policy-making processes.

History

The Indian Statistical Institute was founded in 1931 by Professor P.C. Mahalanobis in Kolkata (then Calcutta). It started in a small room in the Presidency College and later moved to its current location in Baranagar. The institute gained recognition for its pioneering work in statistical methodology and its applications to various fields. In 1959, the Indian Parliament recognized ISI as an Institute of National Importance and authorized it to award degrees. The institute played a crucial role in India's Five-Year Plans through its statistical expertise and contributed to the establishment of the Central Statistical Organization and the National Sample Survey.

Vision and Mission

  • To promote the study and teaching of statistics and related disciplines.
  • To undertake research in various fields of natural and social sciences.
  • To provide training in statistical methods and their applications.
  • To develop statistical theory and methodology for solving real-world problems.

Objectives

  • To advance knowledge in statistical sciences and their applications.
  • To train students in theoretical and applied statistics.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions for research and education.
  • To contribute to national development through statistical research and applications.

🏕 Campus Life

The Indian Statistical Institute has its main campus in Kolkata, spread over 30 acres of lush greenery. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. The institute also has centers in Delhi, Bangalore, Chennai, and Tezpur, each with its own unique academic environment. The Kolkata campus is known for its blend of historic buildings and modern facilities, offering a unique academic experience.

Hostels and Accommodation

ISI provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The institute has several hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and research students. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at ISI is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

ISI encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, tennis courts, table tennis rooms, and a cricket ground. The institute also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year. The proximity to the River Hooghly adds to the scenic beauty of the campus.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at ISI is vibrant and diverse. The institute has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Utsav," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from various institutions and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

ISI provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The institute also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at ISI is one of the best in the country for statistical literature.
