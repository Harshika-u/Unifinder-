Basic Information

Ambedkar University Delhi (AUD) is a state university established by the Government of NCT of Delhi in 2007-2008. The university is named after Dr. B.R. Ambedkar, the architect of the Indian Constitution. AUD is known for its focus on social sciences, humanities, and liberal arts education. The university follows a semester system and offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines. With its commitment to inclusive education and social justice, AUD has emerged as a center of academic excellence in the national capital region.

History

Ambedkar University Delhi was established through an Act of the Delhi Legislature in 2007. The university began functioning from the academic session 2008-09 with its first campus at Kashmere Gate. The university was envisioned as a unique institution that would combine academic excellence with a commitment to social justice and inclusive education. Over the years, AUD has expanded to multiple campuses across Delhi, including Lodhi Road and Karampura. The university has grown steadily in terms of student enrollment, academic programs, and research output, establishing itself as a premier institution for social sciences and humanities education in India.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

The university offers M.Phil. and Ph.D. programs in various disciplines, providing students with the opportunity to work on innovative research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

The research programs at AUD are designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The programs involve coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

🏕 Campus Life

Ambedkar University Delhi operates from multiple campuses across the city, including Kashmere Gate, Lodhi Road, and Karampura. Each campus provides a unique learning environment with modern facilities and infrastructure. The campuses are well-connected by public transport and offer a vibrant academic atmosphere that encourages intellectual growth and social engagement.

Hostels and Accommodation

AUD provides hostel facilities to students at its Karampura campus. The hostels are well-equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, common rooms, and recreational facilities. The university also assists students in finding accommodation in nearby areas for those who cannot get hostel accommodation. The hostel life at AUD is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

AUD encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The university provides sports facilities at its campuses, including indoor and outdoor games. Students participate in various sports activities and tournaments organized at inter-university and national levels. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at AUD is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Srijan," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

AUD provides modern facilities to support academic and research activities. The campuses have well-equipped classrooms, seminar halls, computer labs, and libraries. The university has a central library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. The campuses also have cafeterias, health centers, and other amenities that cater to the needs of students and faculty. The university is committed to providing an accessible and inclusive learning environment for all students.
