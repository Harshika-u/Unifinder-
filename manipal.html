About MAHE

The Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is a private deemed university in the Udupi district of Karnataka, India. The school has campuses in Mangaluru, Bengaluru, and Jamshedpur in India, as well as global campuses in Dubai and Malaysia. Its sister universities are Manipal University Jaipur and Sikkim Manipal University, all managed by the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG).

History

MAHE‚Äôs journey began in 1953 with India‚Äôs first private medical school, followed by Manipal Institute of Technology in 1957. The first private university in India, it became a deemed university in 1993. It is managed by the Manipal Education and Medical Group (MEMG) and was declared an Institution of Eminence in 2020. The campus, spread over 600 acres, has grown into a cosmopolitan education hub attracting students from over 60 countries, renowned for academic innovation, infrastructure, and global partnerships.

Governance

At MAHE, Dr. Ramdas Madhav Pai is the Chancellor. Dr. Ranjan Pai acts as the President, while Dr. Hebri Subhaskrishna Ballal holds the position of Pro Chancellor. The university's governance is overseen by Lt. Gen. (Dr.) M. D. Venkatesh, who serves as the Vice Chancellor.

üìù Admissions ‚Äì Manipal Academy of Higher Education

1. Appear for MET (Manipal Entrance Test)

  • Conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE)
  • Register online, choose preferred exam cities, and pay the application fee
  • MET is the primary entrance exam for B.Tech and other UG programs
1

2. Qualify MET

  • MET is a computer-based test covering Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and English
  • Highly competitive; negative marking applies
2

3. Participate in Counselling

  • Based on MET rank, participate in online counselling
  • Choose preferred branch and campus (Manipal, Jaipur, Sikkim)
3

4. Seat Allotment & Document Verification

  • Seats allotted based on MET rank, category, and branch availability
  • Upload/submit documents for verification (Class 10 & 12 marksheets, MET scorecard, ID proof, etc.)
4

5. Fee Payment & Admission Confirmation

  • Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat
  • Complete hostel and other formalities
5

6. Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum 50% marks in Class 12 (PCM) for General/OBC; 45% for SC/ST/PwD candidates
  • Must have studied Physics, Mathematics, and English in 10+2
  • Maximum of 2 attempts allowed for MET in 2 consecutive years

Note: For other programs (MBBS, MBA, etc.), admissions are through NEET, CAT/XAT, or other national-level entrance exams as applicable.
For more details, visit the official admissions portal: manipal.edu/mu/admissions.html
