Basic Information

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is a prestigious private deemed university located in Manipal, Karnataka. Founded in 1953 by Dr. T.M.A. Pai, MAHE has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution offering programs in medicine, engineering, management, humanities, and more. With state-of-the-art facilities and distinguished faculty, MAHE is consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

Manipal Academy of Higher Education was established in 1953 as Kasturba Medical College, the first private medical college in India. Founded by Dr. T.M.A. Pai, a visionary educationist, the institution grew rapidly with the addition of engineering colleges in 1957 and management programs in 1980. In 1993, it was granted deemed university status and renamed Manipal Academy of Higher Education. Over the years, MAHE has expanded to include campuses in Bangalore, Mangalore, Jaipur, Dubai, Malaysia, and Antigua, becoming a global leader in higher education.

MAHE is renowned for its research programs across various disciplines. The institute offers PhD programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at MAHE is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

🏕 Campus Life

Manipal Academy of Higher Education campus is spread over 600 acres in the coastal town of Manipal, Karnataka. The campus provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its blend of modern infrastructure and green spaces, MAHE offers a unique academic experience in one of India's most beautiful university towns.

Hostels and Accommodation

MAHE provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has several hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and international students, separate for boys and girls. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at MAHE is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

MAHE encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at MAHE is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Revels," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

MAHE provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at MAHE is one of the largest university libraries in India with over 5 lakh volumes.
