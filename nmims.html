Basic Information

Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies (NMIMS) is a private deemed university located in Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It was established in 1981 by the Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal (SVKM), a public charitable trust. NMIMS was granted deemed-to-be university status in 2003 by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The institute has grown from a management institute to a multi-disciplinary university with campuses across India and one in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

History

NMIMS was established in 1981 as Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies with the aim to cater to the growing demand for management education in India. It started with a modest beginning by offering a Masters in Management Studies (MMS) program with just 40 students and four full-time faculty members. In 1994, NMIMS established the School of Distance Learning to provide management education through distance mode. The year 1999 saw the launch of the first full-time MBA program.

In 2003, NMIMS was granted deemed-to-be university status by the UGC, which allowed it to expand its academic offerings. Following this, the institute established several schools including the School of Pharmacy, School of Science, School of Commerce, and School of Economics. In 2006, NMIMS opened its first off-campus center in Bangalore, followed by Hyderabad in 2010, Indore in 2017, and Navi Mumbai in 2017. The institute also established campuses in Shirpur, Dhule (Maharashtra), Chandigarh, and Dhaka (Bangladesh). Today, NMIMS is recognized as one of India's premier educational institutions with 17 constituent schools and over 17,000 students across its campuses.

🏕️ Campus Life

NMIMS Mumbai campus is located in Vile Parle (West), a prime location in Mumbai. The urban campus provides students with access to the city's vibrant culture while offering a conducive learning environment. The campus is well-equipped with modern infrastructure including state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, libraries, and recreational facilities.

Infrastructure and Facilities

The NMIMS Mumbai campus boasts world-class infrastructure designed to enhance the learning experience of students. The campus features modern classrooms with audio-visual aids, well-equipped laboratories for practical learning, and a comprehensive library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

The university provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with amenities like Wi-Fi, laundry, gymnasium, and common rooms. The campus also includes auditoriums for events and seminars, a cafeteria offering a variety of food options, and sports facilities for indoor and outdoor games.

Recent additions to the campus infrastructure include innovation labs, incubation centers for startups, and upgraded IT facilities to support digital learning. The university continuously invests in improving its infrastructure to meet the evolving needs of students and faculty.

Student Life and Activities

Life at NMIMS Mumbai is vibrant and dynamic, with numerous opportunities for students to engage in extracurricular activities. The university has various student clubs and committees covering areas like academics, sports, cultural activities, social service, and entrepreneurship.

The annual cultural festival "Vaayu" is one of the highlights of campus life, attracting participants from colleges across the country. The festival includes events like music, dance, drama, fashion shows, and various competitions. Similarly, "Paragon" is the annual management fest that provides a platform for students to showcase their business acumen.

Sports enthusiasts can participate in various indoor and outdoor sports through the university's sports facilities. NMIMS teams regularly participate in inter-college tournaments and have won several accolades. The university also encourages students to engage in social service through its NSS chapter and other social initiatives.

Industry Interaction and Global Exposure

NMIMS Mumbai emphasizes industry-academia interface through guest lectures, workshops, seminars, and corporate interactions. The university regularly invites industry leaders to share their insights with students, bridging the gap between theory and practice.

The university has collaborations with several international universities, offering student exchange programs, dual degree programs, and study abroad opportunities. These global partnerships provide students with exposure to international business practices and diverse cultural experiences.

The university's placement cell works closely with the industry to facilitate internships and final placements. Regular industry visits, live projects, and internships are integral parts of the curriculum, providing students with hands-on experience and industry exposure.

Support Services

NMIMS Mumbai provides comprehensive support services to ensure the overall well-being of its students. The university has a dedicated counseling center that offers academic, personal, and career counseling to students. The counseling services are designed to help students cope with academic pressures, personal challenges, and career decisions.

The university's health center provides basic medical facilities to students. For specialized medical needs, students are referred to nearby hospitals. The campus also has facilities for differently-abled students, including ramps, elevators, and special washrooms.

The university's mentorship program pairs senior students with juniors to help them navigate campus life and academics. Additionally, the alumni association actively engages with current students, providing mentorship, networking opportunities, and career guidance.
