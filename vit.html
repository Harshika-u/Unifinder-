Basic Information

Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) is a premier private deemed university located in Vellore, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 1984 as Vellore Engineering College, it was granted the status of a deemed university in 2001 by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India. VIT has been consistently ranked among the top engineering institutions in India by various ranking agencies. The university is known for its academic excellence, research infrastructure, and international collaborations. VIT has been accredited with the highest grade of A++ by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India.

History

Vellore Institute of Technology was founded in 1984 by Dr. G. Viswanathan, a former parliamentarian and minister in the Tamil Nadu government, as Vellore Engineering College. The institution started with a modest strength of 180 students and offered three undergraduate engineering programs. Over the years, it has grown into a comprehensive university offering numerous undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines. In 2001, the college was conferred the status of a deemed university by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and was renamed Vellore Institute of Technology. The university has expanded its presence with campuses in Chennai, Bhopal, and Amaravati, apart from its main campus in Vellore.

üèï Campus Life

The VIT campus is spread over 372 acres of lush greenery in Vellore, Tamil Nadu. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its modern infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, VIT offers a unique academic experience.

Hostels and Accommodation

VIT provides on-campus accommodation to all its students. The institute has 18 hostels for men and 6 hostels for women. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at VIT is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

VIT encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, football ground, and athletic tracks. The institute also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at VIT is vibrant and diverse. The institute has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Riviera," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

VIT provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The institute also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias and food courts that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community.
