Basic Information

Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) is a premier central university located in New Delhi, India. Established in 1969 by an act of Parliament, JNU is named after India's first Prime Minister, Jawaharlal Nehru. The university is known for its academic excellence, research output, and strong emphasis on social sciences, humanities, and sciences. With its diverse student community and distinguished faculty, JNU has been consistently ranked among the top universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

Jawaharlal Nehru University was established in 1969 by an act of Parliament with the vision of promoting national integration, social justice, and democratic values. The university was conceived as a center for higher learning that would combine excellence with social relevance. G. Parthasarathy was the first Vice-Chancellor of JNU. Over the years, JNU has evolved into a multi-disciplinary university with a strong focus on research and critical thinking. The university has played a significant role in shaping academic discourse in India and has produced numerous scholars, policymakers, and leaders who have made significant contributions to their respective fields.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a world-class university dedicated to the pursuit of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation.
  • To foster critical thinking, scientific temper, and social responsibility among students.
  • To promote interdisciplinary research and collaborative learning.
  • To contribute to the building of a just, equitable, and pluralistic society.

Objectives

  • To advance knowledge in various disciplines through quality teaching and research.
  • To develop skilled professionals who can contribute to national development.
  • To create an environment that fosters creativity, innovation, and critical thinking.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions for academic and research excellence.
  • To promote inclusive education and social justice.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

JNU is renowned for its research programs across various disciplines. The university offers PhD programs in all its schools and centers, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at JNU is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Social Sciences: Political Studies, Sociology, Economics, History, etc.
  • International Studies: International Relations, Area Studies, etc.
  • Language, Literature & Culture Studies: Various languages and literatures
  • Life Sciences: Molecular Biology, Genetics, Bioinformatics, etc.
  • Physical Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Environmental Sciences, etc.
  • Computer & Systems Sciences: Computer Science, Computational Biology, etc.
  • Arts & Aesthetics: Visual Arts, Performing Arts, etc.

Admission to the PhD program is based on the Jawaharlal Nehru University Entrance Examination (JNUEE) followed by interviews. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support PhD students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

Jawaharlal Nehru University campus is spread over 1019 acres in the southern part of New Delhi. The campus provides a vibrant and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its lush greenery, diverse student community, and rich cultural life, JNU offers a unique academic experience in the heart of India's capital.

Hostels and Accommodation

JNU provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has several hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and research students, with separate arrangements for male and female students. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at JNU is known for its vibrant political and cultural discussions, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and intellectual growth.

Sports and Recreation

JNU encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at JNU is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "North East Festival," celebrates the cultural diversity of India's northeastern states. Other festivals like "Kallol" (Bengali cultural festival), "Umang" (Hindi cultural festival), and "Summit" (international students' festival) showcase the multicultural ethos of the university.

Facilities and Amenities

JNU provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at JNU is one of the largest university libraries in India with over 6 lakh volumes.
