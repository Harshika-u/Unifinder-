Basic Information

Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), formerly known as Manipal University, is a private research and deemed university located in Manipal, Karnataka, India. Established in 1953 by Dr. T.M.A. Pai, MAHE has grown into a multi-disciplinary university with campuses in India and international locations including Dubai, Malaysia, and Nepal. The university is known for its academic excellence, research infrastructure, and international collaborations. MAHE has been accredited with the highest grade of A++ by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India.

History

Manipal Academy of Higher Education was founded in 1953 by Dr. T.M.A. Pai, a renowned physician, educationist, banker, and philanthropist. It began with the establishment of Kasturba Medical College, the first self-financing medical college in the private sector. Over the years, the institution expanded to include various colleges in different disciplines, eventually becoming Manipal University in 1993. In 2007, it was granted the status of a deemed university by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and was renamed Manipal Academy of Higher Education. The university has established campuses in Bangalore, Mangalore, Jamshedpur in India, and in Dubai (UAE), Melaka (Malaysia), and Nepal.
