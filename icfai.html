Basic Information

The ICFAI University is a private university system with multiple campuses across India. Established in 1984, it has grown to become one of the largest private university systems in the country. The university is known for its flexible learning programs and industry-oriented curriculum. It offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines including management, technology, law, and sciences.

History

The ICFAI Group was established in 1984 as a non-profit educational society. The first campus was established in Hyderabad. Over the years, the group has expanded to establish universities in various states including Dehradun, Jaipur, Raipur, Bhubaneswar, Agartala, and Himachal Pradesh. Each university is established under the respective state legislature and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC).

Vision and Mission

  • To be a top-ranking private university of choice for students, faculty and industry.
  • To develop competent professionals with ethical values.
  • To promote research and innovation in all fields of knowledge.

Review 3: Good overall experience

Other: The university provides good exposure through industrial visits and internships. The student diversity is good with students from different parts of the country.

Review 4: Decent university with room for improvement

Placements: The placements are average. While many students get placed, the packages are not very high. The university needs to bring more high-paying companies to campus.
Infrastructure: The infrastructure is good but maintenance could be better. Some labs need upgrading. The hostel rooms are spacious but the furniture is old.
Faculty: The faculty quality varies by department. Some departments have excellent faculty while others need improvement. The teaching methodology is mostly traditional.
