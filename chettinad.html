Basic Information

Chettinad Academy of Research and Education (CARE) is a deemed-to-be university located in Kelambakkam, Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Established in 2005 under the Chettinad Health City, the university has grown into a multi-disciplinary institution offering programs in medical sciences, dental sciences, engineering, management, law, architecture, pharmacy, and arts and sciences. The university is accredited with A Grade by NAAC and recognized by various statutory bodies including MCI, DCI, PCI, COA, and BCI. CARE is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, research facilities, and commitment to providing quality education. The campus is spread across approximately 150 acres and provides a conducive environment for learning and research.

History

The Chettinad Academy of Research and Education (CARE) was established in 2005 by the Chettinad Health City, a initiative of the Rajah Sir Muthiah Chettiar Charitable and Educational Trust. The trust has been involved in educational and healthcare activities since 1956. The university began with the establishment of the Chettinad Hospital and Research Institute (CHRI) in 2005, which was later upgraded to a deemed-to-be university in 2008. Over the years, the university has expanded its academic offerings by establishing various constituent colleges including: - Chettinad College of Nursing (2005) - Chettinad Dental College and Research Institute (2007) - Chettinad College of Pharmacy (2007) - Chettinad College of Physiotherapy (2007) - Chettinad School of Engineering and Technology (2009) - Chettinad School of Law (2018) - Chettinad School of Architecture (2018) - Chettinad College of Arts and Science (2019) Today, CARE stands as a comprehensive university offering education in diverse disciplines with a strong focus on research and innovation.

Vision and Mission

Chettinad Academy of Research and Education (CARE) offers a vibrant campus life that goes beyond academics. The sprawling 150-acre campus provides state-of-the-art facilities and a conducive environment for learning, research, and overall development of students. Infrastructure and Facilities

The campus at CARE is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities. The university has well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, libraries with vast collections of books and journals, and auditoriums for events and seminars. The medical and dental colleges have state-of-the-art hospitals attached to them, providing students with excellent clinical exposure.

The campus also houses separate hostels for boys and girls with all necessary amenities. The hostels provide a comfortable and secure living environment with Wi-Fi connectivity, mess facilities, and recreational areas. The campus has sports facilities including grounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and indoor games like table tennis and chess.

Student Life and Activities

Life at CARE is not just about academics; it's about holistic development. The university encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities and events. There are numerous student clubs and societies catering to diverse interests such as music, dance, drama, literature, photography, and more. These clubs provide a platform for students to showcase their talents and develop new skills.

The university organizes various cultural, technical, and sports events throughout the year. The annual cultural fest 'Chettinad Utsav' is a major attraction that brings together students from various colleges to participate in competitions and performances. The technical fest 'Technotsav' provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and innovations.

Health and Wellness

The well-being of students is a priority at CARE. The campus has a healthcare center with qualified medical professionals to provide medical care to students. The university also has a counseling center to address the mental health needs of students. Regular health check-ups and awareness programs are conducted to promote health and wellness among students.

The campus has a well-maintained cafeteria that serves hygienic and nutritious food. There are also several food courts and cafes on campus that offer a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes. The university promotes a healthy lifestyle by encouraging students to participate in sports and fitness activities.

Community Engagement

Chettinad Academy of Research and Education encourages students to engage with the community through various outreach programs. The National Service Scheme (NSS) and other student organizations conduct regular community service activities in nearby villages and urban areas. These activities include health camps, awareness programs, cleanliness drives, and educational initiatives.

The university also has collaborations with various national and international institutions for academic and research exchange programs. These collaborations provide students with opportunities to gain global exposure and experience different cultures and educational systems.

Placement Overview

Chettinad Academy of Research and Education has a dedicated placement cell that works towards providing excellent career opportunities to students. The university has a strong track record of placements with leading companies and hospitals recruiting students from various disciplines.

The placement cell organizes regular training programs, workshops, and guest lectures by industry experts to enhance the employability of students. It also facilitates internships and industrial visits to provide students with practical exposure and industry insights.

The university has collaborations with various national and international organizations for placements and research opportunities. The alumni network of CARE is also actively involved in mentoring current students and providing placement assistance.
