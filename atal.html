Basic Information

Atal Bihari Vajpayee Institute of Medical Sciences and Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital (ABVIMS & Dr. RML Hospital) is a premier medical institution located in New Delhi, India. Established as an autonomous institute under the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India in 2019, it is named after former Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee and freedom fighter Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia. The institute is affiliated with the National Medical Commission (NMC) and is recognized for providing quality medical education and healthcare services. It operates within the premises of the historic Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital, which has been serving the nation since its establishment in the early 1930s.

History

The Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital, originally known as Willingdon Hospital, was established in the early 1930s with just 54 beds. Over the decades, it has grown into a multi-specialty tertiary care hospital with over 1,500 beds, providing affordable healthcare services to millions of patients. In 2019, the Government of India upgraded the hospital to an autonomous institute and named it Atal Bihari Vajpayee Institute of Medical Sciences and Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Hospital. This transformation aimed to enhance medical education and research while maintaining the hospital's commitment to providing quality healthcare to all sections of society. The institute has since become a center of excellence in medical education, research, and patient care, attracting students and faculty from across the country.

Vision and Mission

  • Vision: To be a globally recognized institution of excellence in medical education, research, and healthcare delivery.
  • Mission: To provide quality medical education, promote research and innovation, and deliver compassionate healthcare services to all sections of society.
  • To develop skilled and ethical healthcare professionals who can serve the nation with dedication and commitment.
  • To foster a culture of research and innovation in medical sciences.
  • To provide affordable and accessible healthcare services to all, especially the underprivileged sections of society.
