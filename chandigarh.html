Basic Information

Chandigarh University (CU) is a private university located in Mohali, Punjab, India. The university was established in 2012 under the Punjab State Legislature Act. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and accredited with an A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). The university campus is spread over 200+ acres of land in Gharuan, Mohali, with state-of-the-art infrastructure and facilities. Chandigarh University has emerged as one of the fastest-growing private universities in India, with a focus on research, innovation, and industry-oriented education.

History

Chandigarh University was established in 2012 by the Punjab State Legislature under "The Chandigarh University Act". The university was founded with a vision to provide quality education and research opportunities to students in India and abroad. Since its inception, the university has grown exponentially, both in terms of infrastructure and academic offerings. Starting with a few courses, Chandigarh University now offers more than 200 programs across various disciplines. The university has established collaborations with over 300 international universities and institutions, providing students with global exposure and opportunities. Over the years, Chandigarh University has received numerous accolades and recognitions, including being ranked among the top private universities in India by various ranking agencies.

  • Pay the application fee (INR 1000 for most courses)

3. Appear for Entrance Exam

  • CUCET (Chandigarh University Common Entrance Test) is conducted multiple times a year
  • Exam pattern varies depending on the program
  • For technical programs, questions are based on Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology
  • For management programs, questions cover English, Reasoning, General Awareness, and Aptitude
3

4. Counseling and Seat Allotment

  • Based on entrance exam rank, candidates are called for counseling
  • Multiple rounds of counseling may be conducted
  • Seat allotment is based on rank, preferences, and availability

5. Document Verification

    • Category certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs
    • Government-issued ID proof

🏕️ Campus Life

Chandigarh University offers a vibrant and diverse campus life that goes beyond academics. Spread over 200+ acres, the campus is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities that provide students with an environment conducive to learning, growth, and overall development. The university believes in holistic education and provides numerous opportunities for students to explore their interests and talents outside the classroom.

Infrastructure and Facilities

The Chandigarh University campus boasts modern infrastructure and world-class facilities. The university has well-equipped laboratories, smart classrooms, a central library with a vast collection of books and digital resources, and dedicated research centers. The campus also includes sports facilities like a cricket ground, football field, basketball courts, tennis courts, and a gymnasium. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls with all necessary amenities including Wi-Fi, mess, laundry, and 24/7 security.

Student Clubs and Societies

Chandigarh University has a wide range of student clubs and societies catering to diverse interests. These include technical clubs, cultural clubs, sports clubs, literary societies, and social service groups. Students can join these clubs based on their interests and participate in various activities and events organized throughout the year. These clubs provide a platform for students to develop leadership skills, pursue their hobbies, and interact with like-minded peers.

Cultural and Technical Festivals

Chandigarh University organizes various cultural and technical festivals throughout the year. The most prominent among them is "CU Fest," which is a week-long celebration of music, dance, drama, and art. The university also hosts technical events like "TechFest" where students showcase their technical skills and innovations. These festivals provide students with opportunities to showcase their talents, learn from industry experts, and network with peers from other institutions.

Sports and Recreation

Sports and physical activities are an integral part of campus life at Chandigarh University. The university encourages students to participate in various sports and games to maintain physical fitness and develop team spirit. The campus has facilities for both indoor and outdoor sports. The university organizes inter-college and inter-university sports competitions regularly. Students can also join the university sports teams and represent the university at national and international levels.
