Basic Information

Chandigarh University (CU) is a leading private university located in Mohali, Punjab. Established in 2012, the university has quickly grown to become one of India's premier institutions for higher education. With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a focus on industry-oriented education, CU has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by various ranking agencies. The university offers a diverse range of programs across multiple disciplines and is known for its strong industry connections and placement record.

History

Chandigarh University was established in 2012 under the Chandigarh University Act by the Punjab State Legislature. The university was founded by S. Satnam Singh Sandhu with the vision of creating a world-class educational institution that would provide quality education and foster innovation. Since its inception, the university has witnessed remarkable growth, expanding its academic offerings, infrastructure, and student population. CU has established collaborations with numerous international universities and industry partners, enhancing its global outlook and providing students with diverse learning opportunities.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a globally recognized institution of excellence in teaching, research, and innovation.
  • To nurture talent and develop future leaders with ethical values and social responsibility.
  • To foster an environment of creativity, critical thinking, and entrepreneurship.

Objectives

  • To provide industry-oriented education that meets global standards.
  • To promote research and innovation across various disciplines.
  • To establish strong industry-academia partnerships for enhanced learning.
  • To create a diverse and inclusive campus community that fosters holistic development.

üèï Campus Life

Chandigarh University campus is spread over 200+ acres in Mohali, Punjab. The campus provides a modern and vibrant environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure, green spaces, and student-friendly facilities, CU offers a holistic learning experience that goes beyond academics.

Hostels and Accommodation

Chandigarh University provides on-campus accommodation to a large number of students. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls with options for AC and non-AC rooms. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, gym, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at CU is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

Chandigarh University encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, football ground, and indoor sports complex. The university also has dedicated spaces for yoga and meditation. Regular sports tournaments and fitness programs are organized throughout the year to promote physical well-being among students.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Chandigarh University is vibrant and diverse. The university has numerous student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "CU Fest," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents. The university also celebrates regional and national festivals with great enthusiasm.

Facilities and Amenities

Chandigarh University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms with smart learning technology. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, shopping complex, food courts, and transportation services. The library at CU is a hub of knowledge and learning, with access to digital resources and study spaces that encourage collaborative learning.
