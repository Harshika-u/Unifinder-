Basic Information

Ashoka University is a pioneering private liberal arts university located in Sonipat, Haryana. Founded in 2014 by a group of entrepreneurs, academics, and philanthropists, Ashoka has quickly established itself as a premier institution of higher education in India. The university is named after Emperor Ashoka, who represents the values of tolerance, pluralism, and non-violence. Ashoka University follows a multidisciplinary approach to education, encouraging students to explore diverse subjects and develop critical thinking skills.

History

Ashoka University was established in 2014 with the vision of creating a world-class liberal arts university in India. The university was founded by a group of prominent individuals including Vineet Gupta, Pramath Raj Sinha, and Ashish Dhawan, among others. The first batch of students was admitted in August 2014, and since then, the university has grown significantly in terms of student strength, faculty, and infrastructure. Ashoka University was built on the philosophy of providing a multidisciplinary education that combines the best of liberal arts and sciences, preparing students to become well-rounded individuals capable of addressing complex global challenges.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a world-class university that provides a multidisciplinary liberal arts education of the highest quality.
  • To foster intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and leadership skills among students.
  • To create a community of learners committed to ethical values and social responsibility.

Objectives

  • To provide a holistic education that combines academic rigor with experiential learning.
  • To promote research and innovation across disciplines.
  • To create a diverse and inclusive campus community that encourages dialogue and exchange of ideas.
  • To develop socially responsible leaders who can contribute to national and global development.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

Ashoka University is committed to promoting research and innovation across disciplines. The university offers PhD programs in various departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at Ashoka University is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Humanities: English, History, Philosophy, Political Science, Sociology
  • Social Sciences: Economics, Psychology, Anthropology
  • Natural Sciences: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics
  • Computer Science and related interdisciplinary areas

Admission to the PhD program is based on a research proposal, interview, and academic record. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support PhD students during their studies.

🏕 Campus Life

Ashoka University's campus is spread over 25 acres in Rajiv Gandhi Education City, Sonipat, Haryana. The campus features modern architecture with state-of-the-art facilities designed to foster learning, creativity, and community engagement. With its blend of contemporary design and green spaces, Ashoka provides an inspiring environment for students, faculty, and staff.

Hostels and Accommodation

Ashoka University provides on-campus accommodation to all undergraduate students. The university has several residence halls that offer comfortable living spaces with modern amenities. Each residence hall has common rooms, study areas, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at Ashoka is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

Ashoka University encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a sports complex with a gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Ashoka University is vibrant and diverse. The university has numerous student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Unmuted," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

Ashoka University provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at Ashoka is a hub of intellectual activity with access to numerous online resources and databases.
