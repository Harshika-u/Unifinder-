Basic Information

The Indian Institute of Management Bangalore (IIMB) is a leading graduate school of management in Asia. Established in 1973, it has since built on its base of highly accomplished faculty, world-class infrastructure and motivated student body to emerge as one of the premier business schools in the world. Under the IIM Act of 2017, IIM Bangalore is declared as an Institute of National Importance (INI).

History

The Indian Institute of Management Bangalore was established in 1973 following the success of IIM Calcutta and IIM Ahmedabad. The institute was built on land donated by the Karnataka government. The first batch of students was admitted in 1973. The institute was initially housed in a rented building on St. Mark's Road before moving to its current campus in 1977. Over the years, IIMB has grown in stature and is now considered one of the premier business schools in India and Asia.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a globally acclaimed management institution that nurtures innovative thinking and leadership.
  • To develop socially conscious leaders, entrepreneurs and managers for facilitating national development.
  • To be a leader in management education, research and consulting.

üéì Courses Offered

IIM Bangalore offers various long-duration executive education programs, a full-time Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP), a Post Graduate Program in Enterprise Management (PGPEM), a Post Graduate Program in Public Policy and Management (PGPPM), a Post Graduate Program in Business Analytics (PGPBA), a Doctoral program (FPM), and various other certificate programs.

Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP)

The two-year full-time Post Graduate Program in Management (PGP) is the flagship program of IIM Bangalore. It is designed to develop professional managers with strong conceptual fundamentals and skills required to manage businesses of the future. The program provides a solid grounding in management theory and practice through case studies, role-plays, business games, and other experiential learning methods.

Program Structure:

The PGP program is structured over six terms, with each term spanning approximately three months. The curriculum consists of core courses in the first year and elective courses in the second year, allowing students to specialize in their areas of interest.

Year Terms Focus
First Year Terms I-III Core Courses
Summer Internship 8-10 weeks Industry Exposure
Second Year Terms IV-VI Elective Courses

At present, approximately 480 students are admitted to the PGP program each year. Admissions are based on performance in the Common Admission Test (CAT), followed by a Written Ability Test (WAT) and Personal Interview (PI). The program has a diverse student body with participants from various academic backgrounds and work experiences.

Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP)

The Executive Post Graduate Program (EPGP) is a one-year full-time residential program designed to enhance skills and capabilities essential for responsible positions at senior management levels. The program is ideal for mid-career executives with substantial work experience looking to transition to senior roles or entrepreneurs seeking to scale their businesses.

üèïÔ∏è Campus Life

The IIM Bangalore campus is spread over 100 acres and is known for its lush greenery and modern infrastructure. The campus provides an ideal environment for learning, with state-of-the-art classrooms, seminar rooms, auditoriums, and discussion rooms. The library is one of the best management libraries in Asia, with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources.

Hostel Life

IIMB provides on-campus accommodation to all students in its well-equipped hostels. Each student is allotted a single room with basic furniture and internet connectivity. The hostels have common rooms, TV rooms, and recreational facilities. The campus also has a gym, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, and facilities for indoor games. The mess serves a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes.

Student Committees and Clubs

Student life at IIMB is vibrant with numerous committees and clubs that cater to diverse interests. These include academic clubs, cultural clubs, sports clubs, and social initiatives. Students actively participate in organizing events, competitions, and festivals. The student-run committees handle various aspects of campus life, including placements, alumni relations, cultural activities, and sports events.

Vista: The Annual Business Festival

Vista is IIMB's flagship international business summit that attracts participants from top business schools across the world. The three-day festival features business competitions, leadership talks, workshops, and cultural events. It provides a platform for students to interact with industry leaders, showcase their skills, and network with peers from other institutions.

Eximius: The Entrepreneurship Summit

Eximius is IIMB's annual entrepreneurship summit that brings together aspiring entrepreneurs, venture capitalists, and industry experts. The event features startup showcases, pitching competitions, mentorship sessions, and talks by successful entrepreneurs. It serves as a launchpad for innovative ideas and fosters the entrepreneurial spirit among students.
