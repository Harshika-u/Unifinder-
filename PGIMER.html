Basic Information

The Postgraduate Institute of Medical Education and Research (PGIMER) is a premier medical and research institution located in Chandigarh, India. It was established in 1962 under the Act of Parliament and is recognized as an Institute of National Importance. PGIMER is known for its excellence in medical education, research, and patient care. The institute provides tertiary level healthcare services to patients from the northern region and beyond.

History

PGIMER was conceived in 1960 as a center of excellence which would endeavor to develop patterns of teaching in postgraduate medical education in as many branches as possible and attempt to produce specialists in several disciplines of medicine. It was also envisaged that these specialists would spread out in the country in various medical colleges and medical institutions and impart medical education of highest standard to the students and set up nucleus of excellence in their own institutions.

The Institute was established in 1962 under the Act of Parliament. It was founded by the then Prime Minister of India, Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru. The Nehru Hospital attached to the Institute started functioning in 1963. The Institute was originally housed in a building known as "Flamingo Hall" in Sector 16, Chandigarh. The foundation stone of the new campus was laid in 1963 and the Institute shifted to its present campus in Sector 12 in 1967.

Vision and Mission

  • To attain self-sufficiency in postgraduate medical education and to meet the country's need for highly qualified medical teachers and specialists in various disciplines
  • To develop a pattern of teaching in postgraduate medical education in all its branches so as to demonstrate a high standard of medical education to all medical colleges and other allied institutions in India
  • To bring together in one place educational facilities of the highest order for the training of personnel in all important branches of health activity

Functions

  • Providing high-quality patient care
  • Undertaking research in various disciplines of medicine
  • Providing teaching and training facilities in various disciplines of medicine
  • Providing facilities for postgraduate and doctoral studies in medicine
  • Providing continuing medical education programs

📝 Admissions – PGIMER Chandigarh

1. Check Eligibility Criteria

  • For MD/MS courses: MBBS degree from a recognized university
  • For DM/MCh courses: MD/MS degree in the respective specialty
  • For MSc courses: Bachelor's degree in relevant field
  • For PhD courses: Postgraduate degree in relevant field
  • Must have completed compulsory rotatory internship
  • Must be registered with Medical Council of India/State Medical Council
1

2. Appear for Entrance Examination

  • For MD/MS/DM/MCh courses: INI CET (Institute of National Importance Combined Entrance Test)
  • For MSc courses: PGIMER entrance exam
  • For PhD courses: PGIMER PhD entrance exam
  • For BSc courses: PGIMER entrance exam
  • Examinations are generally conducted twice a year (January and July sessions)
2

3. Qualify the Entrance Examination

  • Secure a rank above the cutoff for the desired course
  • Cutoff varies for different categories (General, SC/ST/OBC, PwBD)
  • Higher rank increases chances of getting the desired specialty
  • Results are declared on the official PGIMER website
3

4. Participate in Counseling

  • Online counseling is conducted based on merit
  • Fill choices of courses in order of preference
  • Seats are allotted based on rank and availability
  • Multiple rounds of counseling may be conducted
  • Physical reporting may be required after seat allotment
4

5. Document Verification

  • Submit original documents for verification:
    • Entrance examination admit card and scorecard
    • MBBS/MD/MS degree certificate and mark sheets
    • Internship completion certificate
    • Medical Council registration certificate
    • Category certificate (if applicable)
    • Passport-sized photographs
    • Government-issued ID proof
5

6. Fee Payment and Joining

  • Pay the required admission and tuition fees
  • Complete joining formalities
  • Attend orientation program
  • Complete medical fitness examination
  • Complete hostel allocation formalities (if applicable)
  • Commence academic sessions as per schedule
6

Reservation Policy

PGIMER follows the reservation policy as per Government of India norms:

  • Scheduled Castes (SC): 15%
  • Scheduled Tribes (ST): 7.5%
  • Other Backward Classes (OBC): 27%
  • Economically Weaker Sections (EWS): 10%
  • Persons with Benchmark Disabilities (PwBD): 5% (horizontal reservation)

🏕️ Campus Life

The PGIMER campus is spread over 277 acres in Sector 12, Chandigarh. The institute has state-of-the-art infrastructure including advanced research laboratories, well-equipped lecture theaters, modern hospital facilities, and comfortable hostels. The campus provides an ideal environment for learning, research, and patient care.

Hostel Facilities

PGIMER provides hostel facilities to its students. There are separate hostels for male and female students. The hostels are well-furnished with basic amenities including:

  • Spacious rooms with furniture
  • 24/7 water and electricity supply
  • Wi-Fi connectivity
  • Common rooms with television
  • Indoor and outdoor sports facilities
  • Mess facilities
  • Laundry services
  • 24/7 security

The hostel life at PGIMER is vibrant and provides opportunities for interaction with peers from diverse backgrounds. The hostels organize various cultural and sports events throughout the year.

Sports and Recreation

PGIMER encourages students to participate in sports and recreational activities for overall development. The institute has excellent sports facilities including:

  • Basketball courts
  • Volleyball courts
  • Tennis courts
  • Badminton courts
  • Table tennis tables
  • Gymnasium
  • Running tracks
  • Indoor games room

The institute organizes an annual sports meet where students and staff participate in various sports competitions. There are also various clubs and societies that students can join based on their interests.

Cultural Activities

PGIMER has a vibrant cultural scene with various events and festivals organized throughout the year. The institute celebrates national festivals and organizes cultural programs where students showcase their talents in music, dance, drama, and other art forms.

The annual cultural festival "Parikrama" is one of the most awaited events at PGIMER. It includes various competitions, performances, and activities that bring together students, faculty, and staff. The festival provides a platform for students to relax and rejuvenate amidst their rigorous academic schedule.

Student Welfare

PGIMER has a dedicated Student Welfare Committee that looks after the needs and concerns of students. The committee addresses various issues related to academics, accommodation, health, and overall well-being of students.

The institute has a counseling center that provides psychological support to students. Regular health check-ups are conducted for all students. The institute also has a grievance redressal mechanism to address the concerns of students.

Career Opportunities

PGIMER Chandigarh is renowned for producing highly skilled medical professionals who are in great demand across the globe. The institute has a strong alumni network that helps in placement and career guidance. Graduates from PGIMER have excellent career prospects in various sectors.

Career Paths

Placement Support

PGIMER has a dedicated placement cell that assists students in finding suitable career opportunities. The cell organizes campus interviews, job fairs, and career guidance sessions. The institute also has collaborations with various national and international healthcare organizations for recruitment.
