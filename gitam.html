Basic Information

GITAM School of Business (GSB) is a premier constituent of GITAM (Deemed to be University), known for its excellence in commerce and management education. Established in 1980, GSB has grown into a leading business school in India with campuses in Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru. The school is committed to nurturing future business leaders through innovative curriculum, industry exposure, and holistic development.

History

GITAM School of Business traces its origins to 1980 when GITAM was established as a college of engineering. Over the years, the institution expanded its academic offerings and in 2007, GITAM was granted deemed-to-be university status by the Government of India. The School of Business was established as a separate entity to focus on commerce and management education. Since its inception, GSB has been at the forefront of business education in India. The school has consistently evolved its curriculum to meet the changing demands of the industry and has established strong connections with corporate partners. GSB has grown from a single campus in Visakhapatnam to three campuses across India, providing quality education to students from diverse backgrounds.

🏕️ Campus Life

GITAM School of Business offers a vibrant and diverse campus life that fosters holistic development. The campus is a melting pot of cultures with students from all over India and abroad. The university provides state-of-the-art facilities and a nurturing environment for students to pursue their academic interests while engaging in various extracurricular activities.

Infrastructure and Facilities

The GITAM campuses are spread across Visakhapatnam, Hyderabad, and Bengaluru with world-class infrastructure. The commerce and management students have access to modern classrooms, computer labs with latest software, financial trading labs, and well-stocked libraries. The campus also has Wi-Fi connectivity, auditoriums, conference halls, and seminar rooms equipped with audio-visual aids.

The university provides excellent hostel facilities with separate accommodations for boys and girls. The hostels have spacious rooms, mess facilities, common rooms with TVs, and recreational areas. The campus also has cafeterias, food courts, health centers, banking facilities, and shopping complexes for the convenience of students.

Clubs and Committees

GITAM School of Business has a vibrant student community with numerous clubs and committees that cater to diverse interests. The commerce and management students can join clubs like the Finance Club, Marketing Club, Entrepreneurship Cell, and Business Analytics Club. These clubs organize various events, workshops, guest lectures, and competitions throughout the year.

Other popular clubs include the Debating Society, Quiz Club, Photography Club, Music Club, Dance Club, Drama Club, and Sports Club. These clubs provide students with opportunities to pursue their hobbies, develop new skills, and interact with like-minded peers. The university also has student chapters of professional bodies like CII, AIMA, and ISTD.

Festivals and Events

GITAM School of Business is known for its vibrant campus life with numerous festivals and events organized throughout the year. The most popular festival is 'SAMAGAM', the annual cultural and management festival that attracts participants from colleges across the country. The festival includes music, dance, drama, fashion shows, and various management events.

The commerce and management departments organize their own festivals like 'FINOMICS' for finance enthusiasts and 'MARKETRIX' for marketing students. These events include business plan competitions, case study contests, panel discussions with industry leaders, and networking sessions. The university also hosts international conferences, seminars, and workshops on contemporary business issues.

Sports and Recreation

GITAM School of Business encourages sports and physical activities as part of holistic development. The campus has excellent sports facilities including a multipurpose stadium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, volleyball courts, and cricket grounds. The university has teams for various sports that participate in inter-university tournaments.

The campus also has a state-of-the-art fitness center with modern equipment. Students can participate in various sports activities through the Sports Club. The university organizes an annual sports meet 'SPARDHA' where students compete in various athletic events. Adventure activities like trekking, rock climbing, and water sports are also organized regularly.
