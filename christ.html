Basic Information

Christ University is a premier private deemed university located in Bangalore, Karnataka, India. Established in 1969 as Christ College, it was granted the status of a deemed university in 2008 by the University Grants Commission (UGC). The university is managed by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), a Catholic religious congregation known for their commitment to education. Christ University has been consistently ranked among the top private universities in India by various ranking agencies. The university is known for its academic excellence, value-based education, and emphasis on holistic development of students. It has been accredited with an A+ grade by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and has been recognized as an Institution of Eminence by the Government of India.

History

Christ University was founded in 1969 by the Carmelites of Mary Immaculate (CMI), a Catholic religious congregation, as Christ College. The institution started with a vision to provide quality education with a focus on holistic development. Over the years, it has grown into a comprehensive university offering numerous undergraduate, postgraduate, and research programs across various disciplines. In 2008, the college was conferred the status of a deemed university by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and was renamed Christ University. The university has expanded its presence with campuses in Bangalore Central, Bangalore Kengeri, Bangalore Bannerghatta Road, Delhi NCR, and Pune, apart from its main campus in Bangalore Central.

Placement Procedure

The placement process at Christ University follows a systematic approach:

  1. Companies are invited to participate in the placement drive
  2. Job descriptions and eligibility criteria are shared with students
  3. Interested students register for the recruitment process
