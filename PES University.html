Basic Information

People's Education Society University (PESU), is a private university in Bengaluru, India. Established in 1988, it is focused on the following educational areas: Engineering, Management, Law, Economics, Commerce, Architecture, Design, Psychology, Arts, Medicine and Life Sciences. The institution offers both foundation courses in these areas, as well as specializations, with a Bachelor's, Master's, or PhD degree. The college was established as PES University in 2013, as a private university. There are three campuses under PES University in Bangalore: Ring Road Campus located at Banashankari 3rd stage outer ring road Bangalore, Electronic City Campus located at Electronic City hosur road Bangalore, and Hanmumanth Nagar Campus located at Banashankari 1st stage Bangalore.

History

PESU is managed by the People‘s Education Society (PES), which was founded in 1972, in a rented gymnasium in Bengaluru, with around 40 students. PES currently manages over 45 educational programs in Karnataka and neighboring Andhra Pradesh, with a total of over 18,000 students. PESU was started as PESIT in 1988 as the first engineering college under the PES group of institutions. The college was affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belagavi and later became an autonomous college under VTU. The college was established as PES University in 2013 and renamed as PES University.

🏕️ Campus Life

Campus life at PES University is vibrant, competitive, and designed to provide students with a healthy mix of academic stimulation and extracurricular opportunities. The university operates four Bengaluru campuses—each a self-contained educational ecosystem equipped with state-of-the-art classrooms, laboratories, seminar halls, libraries, computer centers, auditoriums, and dedicated sports facilities. Students enjoy world-class infrastructure, WiFi access, digital learning resources, entrepreneurial incubation centers, and dynamic research spaces. PESU encourages an active campus culture, hosting numerous student-run clubs and societies spanning technology, literature, performing arts, entrepreneurship, social service, photography, debate, and more. The annual fests, Atmatrisha (Ring Road campus) and Maaya (Electronic City campus), bring together thousands of students for cultural competitions, celebrity performances, workshops, hackathons, and social causes. Inter-campus participation is common, fostering camaraderie and a healthy sense of competition.Residential students benefit from modern hostels that prioritize comfort, safety, and a sense of community. Mess halls, cafeterias, and food courts cater to diverse culinary needs. Recreational offerings are abundant, with sports tournaments in cricket, football, basketball, and athletics, as well as wellness amenities like gymnasiums and yoga rooms. Support services such as on-campus counseling, academic mentoring, and student-run help desks contribute to a nurturing environment. The university runs a robust alumni network, career services, and student newsletters that keep Pesuvians informed and inspired. Despite the competitive ethos and occasional academic pressure, the emphasis on innovation, hands-on learning, and leadership sets PESU apart as a transformative space for personal growth and professional preparation.

💼 Placements

Placement Highlights (2024-2025)

PES University is known for its exceptional placement record, with more than 150 companies visiting the campus annually. The university’s proactive Training & Placement Cell works year-round to facilitate campus interviews, internships, career guidance workshops, and industry seminars. Major recruiters include top industry names like Microsoft, Amazon, Google, Cisco, IBM, and Deloitte. Placement statistics are impressive—departments like Computer Science routinely approach 100% placement rates. The highest package in 2024 reached ₹1.5 crore (international posting), with the domestic highest at ₹65 lakh per annum; average packages for core branches like CSE hover around ₹16 lakh, while median packages are substantial across MBA, BTech, and MTech programs (median MBA: ₹5.6 lakh, BTech: ₹12.5 lakh as per NIRF data 2025). PESU students benefit from a curriculum embedded with industry requirements, real-world projects, internships with monthly stipends (₹20,000–80,000), and exposure to emerging technologies like AI and data science. The Career Services team organizes resume-building sessions, mock interviews, and connects students to research opportunities and entrepreneurship cells. The university fosters a professional culture from day one, preparing students for both Indian and global job markets. Alumni working at leading multinational firms frequently return to mentor, network, and expand opportunities for current students. Overall, PES graduates are highly sought after due to their technical acumen and versatility, making placements a standout feature of their academic journey.
