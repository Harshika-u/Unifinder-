Basic Information

Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS) is a deemed-to-be university located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. Established in 2005, it has grown into a premier institution offering education in various fields including medical sciences, dental sciences, engineering, management, law, and other disciplines. The university is known for its state-of-the-art infrastructure, research facilities, and commitment to excellence in education. SIMATS is accredited with A++ grade by NAAC, which is the highest possible grade, making it one of the top institutions in India. The university operates under the Saveetha Medical and Educational Trust, which has been dedicated to providing quality education since 1988. The campus is spread across approximately 180 acres and provides a conducive environment for learning, research, and overall development of students.

History

The Saveetha Medical and Educational Trust was established in 1988 with the vision of providing quality education in various fields. The trust began its journey with the establishment of Saveetha Dental College in 1988, which was followed by Saveetha College of Nursing in 1992. In 2005, the Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS) was established as a deemed-to-be university under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. This marked a significant milestone in the institution's journey, allowing it to expand its academic offerings and research activities. Over the years, SIMATS has grown exponentially, adding new colleges and programs to its portfolio. The Saveetha Medical College was established in 2005, followed by Saveetha Engineering College in 2006, Saveetha School of Management in 2008, Saveetha School of Law in 2018, and several other institutions. Today, SIMATS stands as a multi-disciplinary university with a strong focus on research, innovation, and holistic education, making it one of the most sought-after institutions in India.

🏕️ Campus Life

Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (SIMATS) offers a vibrant campus life that goes beyond academics. The sprawling 180-acre campus provides state-of-the-art facilities and a conducive environment for learning, research, and overall development of students. The campus is designed to meet the needs of modern education while maintaining a serene and eco-friendly atmosphere.

Infrastructure and Facilities

The campus at SIMATS is equipped with modern infrastructure and facilities to support academic and extracurricular activities. The university has well-equipped classrooms, advanced laboratories, libraries with vast collections of books and journals, and auditoriums for events and seminars. The medical and dental colleges have state-of-the-art hospitals attached to them, providing students with excellent clinical exposure.

The campus also houses separate hostels for boys and girls with all necessary amenities. The hostels provide a comfortable and secure living environment with Wi-Fi connectivity, mess facilities, and recreational areas. The campus has sports facilities including grounds for cricket, football, basketball, volleyball, and indoor games like table tennis and chess.

Student Life and Activities

Life at SIMATS is not just about academics; it's about holistic development. The university encourages students to participate in various extracurricular activities and events. There are numerous student clubs and societies catering to diverse interests such as music, dance, drama, literature, photography, and more. These clubs provide a platform for students to showcase their talents and develop new skills.

The university organizes various cultural, technical, and sports events throughout the year. The annual cultural fest 'Kuruksastra' is a major attraction that brings together students from various colleges to participate in competitions and performances. The technical fest 'Sangamam' provides a platform for students to showcase their technical skills and innovations.

Health and Wellness

The well-being of students is a priority at SIMATS. The campus has a healthcare center with qualified medical professionals to provide medical care to students. The university also has a counseling center to address the mental health needs of students. Regular health check-ups and awareness programs are conducted to promote health and wellness among students.

The campus has a well-maintained cafeteria that serves hygienic and nutritious food. There are also several food courts and cafes on campus that offer a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes. The university promotes a healthy lifestyle by encouraging students to participate in sports and fitness activities.

Community Engagement

Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences encourages students to engage with the community through various outreach programs. The National Service Scheme (NSS) and other student organizations conduct regular community service activities in nearby villages and urban areas. These activities include health camps, awareness programs, cleanliness drives, and educational initiatives.

The university also has collaborations with various national and international institutions for academic and research exchange programs. These collaborations provide students with opportunities to gain global exposure and experience different cultures and educational systems.

Placement Overview

Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences has a dedicated placement cell that works towards providing excellent career opportunities to students. The university has a strong track record of placements with leading companies and hospitals recruiting students from various disciplines.

The placement cell organizes regular training programs, workshops, and guest lectures by industry experts to enhance the employability of students. It also facilitates internships and industrial visits to provide students with practical exposure and industry insights.

The university has collaborations with various national and international organizations for placements and research opportunities. The alumni network of SIMATS is also actively involved in mentoring current students and providing placement assistance.
