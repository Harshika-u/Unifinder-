Basic Information

The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade (IIFT) is a premier autonomous institution established in 1963 by the Government of India. In 2002, IIFT was granted the status of Deemed to be University. The institute is dedicated to professionalizing the country's foreign trade management through its teaching, training, and research activities. With its main campus in New Delhi and additional campuses in Kolkata and Kakinada, IIFT has been consistently ranked among the top business schools in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

The Indian Institute of Foreign Trade was established in 1963 as an autonomous body under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry, Government of India. The institute was founded with the objective of professionalizing India's foreign trade management and increasing exports by developing human resources, generating, analyzing, and disseminating data, and conducting research. In 2002, IIFT was granted the status of Deemed to be University by the University Grants Commission (UGC). Over the years, IIFT has expanded its operations with the establishment of additional campuses in Kolkata (2006) and Kakinada (2012). The institute has played a pivotal role in shaping India's trade policies and has been a center of excellence in international business education.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a center of excellence in international business education and research.
  • To develop competent professionals who can contribute effectively to the global business environment.
  • To foster research and consultancy in areas related to international trade and business.

Objectives

  • To provide high-quality education in international business and foreign trade.
  • To conduct research and disseminate knowledge in the field of international business.
  • To offer training programs for industry professionals and government officials.
  • To provide policy inputs to the Government of India on international trade matters.

DOCTORAL PROGRAM

IIFT offers a Ph.D. program in International Business for students interested in pursuing research in the field. The program is designed to develop researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in international business and trade.

Ph.D. Program:

The Ph.D. program at IIFT is a full-time research program that enables students to conduct in-depth research in various areas of international business. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • International Marketing
  • International Finance
  • International Trade Operations and Logistics
  • Global Business Strategy
  • Foreign Direct Investment
  • International Trade Laws and Policies
  • Cross-Cultural Management

Admission to the Ph.D. program is based on a written entrance examination followed by an interview. The institute provides fellowships to support Ph.D. scholars during their studies.

üèï Campus Life

IIFT has three campuses across India - Delhi, Kolkata, and Kakinada. The Delhi campus, the oldest and largest, is spread over 6 acres in the heart of the city. The Kolkata campus is located in Alipore and spans 7 acres. The Kakinada campus, the newest, is spread over 15 acres in Andhra Pradesh. Each campus provides a conducive learning environment with modern infrastructure and facilities.

Hostels and Accommodation

IIFT provides hostel facilities to students at all its campuses. The hostels are well-equipped with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, common rooms, and recreational facilities. The Delhi campus has separate hostels for boys and girls, while the Kolkata and Kakinada campuses have integrated hostel facilities. The hostel life at IIFT is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

IIFT encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campuses have sports facilities including indoor and outdoor games. Students participate in various sports activities and tournaments organized at inter-university and national levels. The Delhi campus has a gymnasium, while the Kolkata and Kakinada campuses have multi-purpose sports halls.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at IIFT is vibrant and diverse. The institute has several student clubs and committees that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Quo Vadis," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, and various competitions. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

IIFT provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campuses have well-equipped classrooms, seminar halls, computer labs, and libraries. The Delhi campus has a dedicated library with a vast collection of books, journals, and digital resources. The institute also has a trading lab that simulates real-time trading environments for students. The campuses also have cafeterias, health centers, and other amenities that cater to the needs of students and faculty.
