Basic Information

SRM School of Management (SRM SoM) is a premier business school under SRM Institute of Science and Technology (SRMIST), a deemed-to-be university located in Kattankulathur, Tamil Nadu. Established in 2007, SRM SoM has quickly emerged as one of the leading management institutions in India, known for its industry-oriented curriculum and strong corporate connections.

History

SRM School of Management was established in 2007 as part of SRM Institute of Science and Technology's initiative to provide quality management education. Since its inception, the school has grown rapidly in terms of infrastructure, faculty strength, and industry collaborations. The school is housed in a dedicated building within the sprawling SRMIST campus, providing state-of-the-art facilities for management education.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a globally recognized business school known for excellence in management education and research.

  • To foster industry-academia collaboration for creating impactful management solutions.

üéì Courses Offered

SRM School of Management offers a range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in management. The curriculum is designed to meet industry requirements and includes practical exposure through internships, live projects, and industry interactions. The school follows a trimester system for its MBA program.

Master of Business Administration (MBA)

The two-year full-time MBA program is the flagship program of SRM SoM. It offers specialization in various functional areas of management. The program includes a summer internship after the first year and provides opportunities for international exchange programs with partner universities abroad.

The MBA program at SRM SoM is known for its industry-oriented curriculum and strong placement record. Students undergo rigorous training in management concepts and practices through case studies, simulations, and real-world projects.

üèïÔ∏è Campus Life

SRM School of Management is part of the larger SRM Institute of Science and Technology campus, which spans 250 acres in Kattankulathur, Tamil Nadu. The campus is a self-contained township with world-class infrastructure, including academic buildings, hostels, sports facilities, shopping complexes, and healthcare facilities. The management school has its own dedicated building with modern classrooms, seminar halls, computer labs, and a library.

Hostel Life

SRM School of Management provides on-campus accommodation to its students in well-equipped hostels. The hostels offer single, double, and triple occupancy rooms with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, air conditioning, and attached bathrooms. Each hostel has common rooms with TV, indoor games, and reading areas. The campus has separate hostels for boys and girls with 24/7 security. The mess facilities offer a variety of cuisines to cater to diverse tastes.

Student Clubs and Committees

SRM School of Management has a vibrant student community with numerous clubs and committees that provide opportunities for extracurricular engagement and leadership development. These student-run organizations cover various domains including finance, marketing, entrepreneurship, cultural activities, and sports. The management school has its own student council that coordinates activities and represents student interests.

Annual Festivals

SRM School of Management organizes several annual festivals and events that provide platforms for students to showcase their talents and skills. The management fest "Milan" is a major inter-college event that attracts participants from business schools across the country. The festival includes business competitions, cultural events, workshops, and guest lectures by industry leaders. The school also participates in the larger SRM University festivals like "Aaruush" (technical fest) and "Tatva" (cultural fest).

Sports and Recreation

The SRM campus has excellent sports facilities that management students can access. These include a multi-purpose stadium, indoor sports complex, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, and cricket grounds. The management school organizes inter-hostel and inter-college sports tournaments throughout the year. The campus also has a fully equipped gymnasium and fitness centers for students to maintain their physical well-being.

SRM School of Management Placements 2023 Report

The school has consistently achieved good placement records with students being recruited by leading companies across various sectors. The placement process includes pre-placement talks, written tests, group discussions, and personal interviews.
