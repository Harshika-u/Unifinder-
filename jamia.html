Basic Information

Jamia Millia Islamia is a premier central university located in New Delhi, India. Founded in 1920 during the British colonial rule, it was established by nationalist Muslim leaders to provide modern education to Indian Muslims. The university has evolved into a multi-faceted institution that offers education in diverse fields while maintaining its commitment to inclusive and secular values. Jamia has been consistently ranked among the top universities in India by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF).

History

Jamia Millia Islamia was originally established at Aligarh in United Provinces, India in 1920. It became a Central University by an act of the Indian Parliament in 1988. The journey of Jamia Millia Islamia has been one of struggle and resilience. In the face of tremendous odds and challenges, the university has emerged as a symbol of educational excellence, secular values, and national integration. The founders of Jamia included prominent nationalist leaders like Dr. Zakir Husain, Maulana Mahmud Hasan, Maulana Mohamed Ali Jauhar, and Hakim Ajmal Khan, who envisioned an institution that would combine modern education with Islamic values.

Vision and Mission

  • To be a leading academic institution that contributes to the building of a just, equitable, and pluralistic society.
  • To provide quality education that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and ethical values.
  • To promote research and innovation that addresses contemporary challenges and contributes to national development.

Objectives

  • To disseminate knowledge and provide educational opportunities to all sections of society, especially the marginalized.
  • To develop skilled professionals who can contribute to national and global development.
  • To create an environment that fosters intellectual growth, cultural diversity, and social harmony.
  • To collaborate with national and international institutions for academic excellence and research.

RESEARCH PROGRAMS

Jamia Millia Islamia is renowned for its research programs across various disciplines. The university offers PhD programs in all departments, providing students with the opportunity to work on cutting-edge research projects under the guidance of experienced faculty members.

PhD Programs:

The PhD program at Jamia is designed to train students to become independent researchers who can contribute to the advancement of knowledge in their chosen fields. The program involves coursework, comprehensive examinations, and original research leading to a thesis.

Research Areas:

  • Engineering: Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics & Communication, Computer Science
  • Social Sciences: Economics, Political Science, Sociology, Psychology, Education
  • Humanities: English, Hindi, Urdu, History, Islamic Studies
  • Natural Sciences: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Biosciences, Biotechnology
  • Management: Business Administration, Commerce

Admission to the PhD program is based on the entrance test conducted by Jamia, followed by interviews. The university provides fellowships and research assistantships to support PhD students during their studies.

üèï Campus Life

Jamia Millia Islamia campus is spread over 253 acres in the national capital, New Delhi. The campus provides a serene and intellectually stimulating environment for students, faculty, and researchers. With its blend of historic buildings and modern facilities, Jamia offers a unique academic experience in one of India's most vibrant cities.

Hostels and Accommodation

Jamia provides on-campus accommodation to most of its students. The university has several hostels for undergraduate, postgraduate, and research students, separate for boys and girls. Each hostel has its own mess, common room, and recreational facilities. The hostels are equipped with Wi-Fi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The hostel life at Jamia is vibrant, with students from diverse backgrounds and cultures living together, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.

Sports and Recreation

Jamia encourages students to maintain a healthy balance between academics and physical activities. The campus has excellent sports facilities, including a stadium, gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, cricket ground, and football ground. The university also has a student activity center where various cultural and recreational activities are organized throughout the year.

Cultural Activities

Cultural life at Jamia is vibrant and diverse. The university has several student clubs and societies that organize various cultural events throughout the year. The annual cultural festival, "Jashn-e-Jamia," is one of the most anticipated events on campus, featuring music, dance, drama, art, and literature. The festival attracts participants from colleges across the country and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Facilities and Amenities

Jamia provides state-of-the-art facilities to support academic and research activities. The campus has well-equipped laboratories, advanced research centers, a central library with a vast collection of books and journals, and modern classrooms. The university also has a health center, banking facilities, post office, shopping complex, and several cafeterias that cater to the diverse culinary preferences of the campus community. The library at Jamia is one of the largest university libraries in India with over 6 lakh volumes.
