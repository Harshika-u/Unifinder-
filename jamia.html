Basic Information

Jamia Millia Islamia is a premier central university located in New Delhi, India. Originally established at Aligarh in United Provinces, India during British rule in 1920, it became a Central University by an act of the Indian Parliament in 1988. The university was founded by nationalist Muslim leaders in response to the call of Mahatma Gandhi and Maulana Mohammad Ali Jauhar to boycott British-supported educational institutions. The university has been consistently ranked among the top universities in India by NIRF and was awarded A++ grade by NAAC in 2021, making it one of the few universities to achieve this distinction.

History

Jamia Millia Islamia was founded in 1920 during the Khilafat and Non-Cooperation Movement in response to Mahatma Gandhi's call for boycotting British-supported institutions. The foundation stone was laid by Sheikhul Hind Maulana Mahmood Hasan. The university was initially established in Aligarh but was moved to Delhi in 1925 after facing opposition from the authorities at Aligarh Muslim University.

The university faced severe financial difficulties in its early years. Hakim Ajmal Khan, Dr. Mukhtar Ahmed Ansari, and Abdul Majeed Khwaja were among the prominent leaders who supported Jamia during this period. In 1962, the University Grants Commission declared Jamia a deemed university. On December 26, 1988, the Jamia Millia Islamia Act was passed by Parliament, granting it the status of a central university.

Over the years, Jamia has grown from a small educational institution to a full-fledged university with various faculties and centers of learning. It has maintained its commitment to providing education that combines modern knowledge with Islamic values while promoting national integration and secularism.

🏕️ Campus Life

Jamia Millia Islamia boasts a vibrant and diverse campus life that enriches the overall educational experience of its students. Spread across approximately 253 acres, the campus provides a perfect blend of academic rigor and extracurricular activities. The university's infrastructure includes state-of-the-art academic buildings, well-equipped laboratories, modern libraries, sports facilities, and student hostels.

Hostel Life

Jamia Millia Islamia provides hostel accommodation to a significant number of students. The university has separate hostels for boys and girls with well-furnished rooms, mess facilities, and common areas for recreation. The hostels create a home away from home and foster a sense of community among students from diverse backgrounds. Each hostel has its own culture and traditions, with various events and activities organized throughout the year.

The university also has a dedicated International House for foreign students, providing them with a comfortable living environment and facilities to help them adjust to life in India. The hostel life at Jamia is known for its inclusivity, cultural exchange, and lifelong friendships formed among residents.

Cultural and Extracurricular Activities

Jamia Millia Islamia has a rich tradition of cultural and extracurricular activities. The university has numerous student clubs and societies catering to various interests, including debate, drama, music, dance, photography, literature, and social service. These clubs provide students with opportunities to explore their talents, develop leadership skills, and engage with like-minded peers.

The university organizes several annual festivals and events, the most prominent being "Jashn-e-Jamia," a cultural festival that showcases the diverse talents of students. Other notable events include sports tournaments, academic conferences, and seminars by eminent personalities. These activities play a crucial role in the holistic development of students and contribute to the vibrant campus atmosphere.

Sports Facilities

Jamia Millia Islamia places great emphasis on sports and physical fitness. The university has excellent sports facilities, including a cricket ground, football fields, basketball courts, tennis courts, volleyball courts, and an indoor sports complex. The university also has a well-equipped gymnasium and facilities for indoor games like table tennis, badminton, and chess.

The university encourages students to participate in sports and regularly organizes inter-departmental and inter-university tournaments. Many students have represented Jamia at national and international levels in various sports. The university's sports teams have achieved notable success in competitions across the country.

Library and Learning Resources

The Dr. Zakir Husain Library, the central library of Jamia Millia Islamia, is a treasure trove of knowledge with a vast collection of books, journals, manuscripts, and digital resources. The library has a collection of over 4 lakh books and subscribes to numerous national and international journals. It also provides access to various e-resources and databases to support research and learning.

In addition to the central library, most faculties have their own departmental libraries catering to the specific needs of their students and faculty. The university also has a modern language lab, computer labs, and other learning resources to facilitate academic excellence.

Jamia Millia Islamia Placements

The Training and Placement Office (TPO) at Jamia Millia Islamia plays a crucial role in facilitating campus placements for students. The university has a dedicated placement cell that works tirelessly to bridge the gap between academia and industry. The TPO organizes various activities throughout the year, including pre-placement talks, workshops, seminars, and training programs to enhance the employability of students.
