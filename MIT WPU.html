Basic Information

MIT World Peace University,(MIT-WPU) is a private university located in Kothrud, Pune, India.It is a part of the MIT Group of Institutions.It is officially named Vishwanath Karad MIT World Peace University. It was established under the Government of Maharashtra Act No. XXXV 2017 and recognized by the University Grants Commission

History

MIT World Peace University traces its origins to the visionary efforts of Dr. Vishwanath Karad, who founded the Maharashtra Institute of Technology in 1983 under the Maharashtra Academy of Engineering and Educational Research (MAEER). MIT was among the pioneers in private engineering education in Maharashtra. Over the years, the institution expanded by establishing various colleges in engineering, medical, management, arts, commerce, and peace studies. A significant transformation occurred in 2017, when MIT was granted private university status, evolving into MIT World Peace University (MIT-WPU). Today, it stands as a comprehensive, globally recognized university committed to holistic development, innovation, and world peace.
